      <h2>suffuse</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : spread through or over (with a color or liquid); charge; Ex. A blush suffused her cheeks.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to spread or flush or flood through, over, or across
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/perfuse">perfuse</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The sky was suffused with a warm pink color               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) to become overspread as with a fluid, a colour, a gleam of light
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
