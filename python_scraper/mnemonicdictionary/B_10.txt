      <h2>belligerent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : quarrelsome</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who fights (or is fighting)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/battler">battler</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/combatant">combatant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fighter">fighter</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scrapper">scrapper</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characteristic of an enemy or one eager to fight
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aggressive">aggressive</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  aggressive acts against another country               </li>
                              <li>
                  a belligerent tone               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) engaged in war
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/militant">militant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/war-ridden">war-ridden</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/warring">warring</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  belligerent (or warring) nations               </li>
                           </ul>
