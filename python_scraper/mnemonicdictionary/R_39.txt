      <h2>rhetoric</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : art of effective communication; art of using language effectively and persuasively; style of speaking or writing; grandiloquent language; Ex. political rhetoric; ADJ. rhetorical; CF. rhetorical questi</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) using language effectively to please or persuade
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) high-flown style; excessive use of verbal ornamentation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grandiloquence">grandiloquence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grandiosity">grandiosity</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/magniloquence">magniloquence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ornateness">ornateness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the grandiosity of his prose               </li>
                              <li>
                  an excessive ornateness of language               </li>
                           </ul>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) loud and confused and empty talk

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/empty talk">empty talk</a>
