      <h2>trek</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : travel; journey; V: make a long difficult journey</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a journey by ox wagon (especially an organized migration by a group of settlers)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any long and difficult trip
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) journey on foot, especially in the mountains
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  We spent the summer trekking in the foothills of the Himalayas               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make a long and difficult journey
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  They trekked towards the North Pole with sleds and skis               </li>
                           </ul>
