      <h2>realm</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a domain in which something is dominant
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/kingdom">kingdom</a>

         </div>
