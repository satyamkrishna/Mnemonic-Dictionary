      <h2>recalcitrant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : disobedient or resisting authority even after being punished; obstinately stubborn; determined to resist authority; unruly; Ex. recalcitrant child</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) stubbornly resistant to authority or control
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fractious">fractious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/refractory">refractory</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a fractious animal that would not submit to the harness               </li>
                              <li>
                  a refractory child               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by stubborn resistance to authority
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the University suspended the most recalcitrant demonstrators               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
