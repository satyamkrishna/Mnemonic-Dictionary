         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone devoted to the promotion of human welfare and to social reforms
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/do-gooder">do-gooder</a>

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/humanist">humanist</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>humdrum</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : dull; monotonous</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the quality of wearisome constancy, routine, and lack of variety
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/monotony">monotony</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sameness">sameness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he had never grown accustomed to the monotony of his work               </li>
                              <li>
                  he was sick of the humdrum of his fellow prisoners               </li>
