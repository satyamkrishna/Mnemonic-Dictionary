      <h2>impotent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : weak; ineffective; lacking in physical strength or power</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking power or ability
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>

                  felt impotent rage               </li>

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
