      <h2>concession</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : an act of yielding; conceding; something conceded; point, right, etc. given unwillingly; privilege of maintaining a business in a certain place; Ex. oil concessions in the North sea; CF. concessionair</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a contract granting the right to operate a subsidiary business
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grant">grant</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of conceding or yielding
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conceding">conceding</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/yielding">yielding</a>

            
             
         </div>
