      <h2>halting</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hesitant; faltering; not fluent; Ex. halting steps/voice; V. halt: proceed or act with uncertainty; falter; hesitate; waver; stop</p>

         <u>Definition</u><br />
