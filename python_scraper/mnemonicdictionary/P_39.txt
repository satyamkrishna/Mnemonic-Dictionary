      <h2>ponderous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : having great weight; weighty; unwieldy; lacking lightness; dull; Ex. ponderous body/style of writing</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) slow and laborious because of weight
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/heavy">heavy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lumbering">lumbering</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the heavy tread of tired troops               </li>
                              <li>
                  moved with a lumbering sag-bellied trot               </li>
                              <li>
                  ponderous prehistoric beasts               </li>
                              <li>
                  a ponderous yawn               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having great mass and weight and unwieldiness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a ponderous stone               </li>
                              <li>
                  a ponderous burden               </li>
                              <li>
                  ponderous weapons               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) labored and dull
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a ponderous speech               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

      <h2>pontifical</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pertaining to a bishop or pope; pompous or pretentious; CF. pontiff: pope; bishop</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the vestments and other insignia of a pontiff (especially a bishop)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>pore</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : study industriously; ponder; scrutinize; Ex. pore over the book; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any tiny hole admitting passage of a liquid (fluid or gas)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any small opening in the skin or outer surface of an animal
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a minute epidermal pore in a leaf or stem through which gases and water vapor can pass
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
