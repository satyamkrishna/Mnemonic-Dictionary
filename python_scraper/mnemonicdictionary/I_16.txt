      <h2>impute</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : attribute; ascribe; charge; N. imputation</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) attribute or credit to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ascribe">ascribe</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/assign">assign</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/attribute">attribute</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  We attributed this quotation to Shakespeare               </li>
                              <li>
                  People impute great cleverness to cats               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) attribute (responsibility or fault) to a cause or source
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The teacher imputed the student's failure to his nervousness               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
