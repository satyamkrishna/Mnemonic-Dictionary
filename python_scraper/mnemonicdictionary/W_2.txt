      <h2>waive</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : give up temporarily; yield; N. waiver: waiving a right or claim; document that waives a right or claim</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) do without or cease to hold or adhere to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dispense with">dispense with</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forego">forego</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/foreswear">foreswear</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forgo">forgo</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/relinquish">relinquish</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  We are dispensing with formalities               </li>
                              <li>
                  relinquish the old ideas               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) lose (s.th.) or lose the right to (s.th.) by some error, offense, or crime
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forego">forego</a>

         </div>
