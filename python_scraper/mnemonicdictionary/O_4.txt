      <h2>obsequious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : slavishly attentive; servile; full of servile compliance; sycophantic</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) attempting to win favor from influential people by flattery
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bootlicking">bootlicking</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fawning">fawning</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sycophantic">sycophantic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/toadyish">toadyish</a>

            
             
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) attentive in an ingratiating or servile manner
