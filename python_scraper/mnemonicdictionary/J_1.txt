      <h2>jab</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : poke abruptly with something sharp; punch with short blows</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sharp hand gesture (resembling a blow)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jabbing">jabbing</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/poke">poke</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/poking">poking</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/thrust">thrust</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/thrusting">thrusting</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he warned me with a jab with his finger               </li>
                              <li>
                  he made a thrusting motion with his fist               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a quick short straight punch
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of touching someone suddenly with your finger or elbow
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dig">dig</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  she gave me a sharp dig in the ribs               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) poke or thrust abruptly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dig">dig</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/poke">poke</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prod">prod</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stab">stab</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he jabbed his finger into her ribs               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) strike or punch with quick and short blows
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) stab or pierce
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stab">stab</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he jabbed the piece of meat with his pocket knife               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
