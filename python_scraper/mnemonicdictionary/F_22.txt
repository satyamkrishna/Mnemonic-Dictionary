      <h2>foolhardy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : rash; reckless; foolishly daring</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by defiant disregard for danger or consequences
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/heady">heady</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rash">rash</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reckless">reckless</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  foolhardy enough to try to seize the gun from the hijacker               </li>
                              <li>
                  became the fiercest and most reckless of partisans               </li>
                              <li>
                  a reckless driver               </li>
                              <li>
                  a rash attempt to climb Mount Everest               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
