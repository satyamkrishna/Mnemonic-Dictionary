      <h2>shoddy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : made of inferior material; sham; not genuine; inferior; dishonest; Ex. shoddy goods/trick</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) reclaimed wool fiber
