      <h2>salubrious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : healthful; conducive to health or well-being; socially desirable; Ex. salubrious area; CF. health</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) promoting health; healthful
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/good for you">good for you</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/healthy">healthy</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>salutary</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : tending to improve; beneficial; wholesome; Ex. The punishment had a salutary effect on the boy; CF. health</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) tending to promote physical well-being; beneficial to health
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/good">good</a>

            
             
         </div>
