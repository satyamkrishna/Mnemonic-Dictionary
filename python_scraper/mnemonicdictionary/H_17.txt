      <h2>hover</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hang about; (of birds or aircraft) stay in the air in one place; (of people) wait nearby; stay around one place; waver; be in an uncertain state</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) be undecided about something; waver between conflicting positions or courses of action
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/oscillate">oscillate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vacillate">vacillate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vibrate">vibrate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He oscillates between accepting the new position and retirement               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move to and fro
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/linger">linger</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) hang in the air; fly or be suspended above
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
