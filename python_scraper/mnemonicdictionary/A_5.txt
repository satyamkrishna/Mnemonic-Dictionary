      <h2>abscond</h2>

      <h2>abstemious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sparing in eating and drinking; temperate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) sparing in consumption of especially food and drink
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the pleasures of the table, never of much consequence to one naturally abstemious               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by temperance in indulgence
