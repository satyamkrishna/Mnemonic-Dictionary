      <h2>truncate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : cut the top off; shorten</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) replace a corner by a plane
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
