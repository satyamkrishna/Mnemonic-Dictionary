      <h2>perverse</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : purposely continuing to do something wrong; stubbornly wrongheaded; perverted; directed away from what is right; wicked and unacceptable; Ex. perverse satisfaction; Ex. Hannibal Lecter in a perverse m</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by a disposition to oppose and contradict
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  took perverse satisfaction in foiling her plans               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) resistant to guidance or discipline
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/contrary">contrary</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/obstinate">obstinate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/wayward">wayward</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Mary Mary quite contrary               </li>
                              <li>
                  an obstinate child with a violent temper               </li>
                              <li>
                  a perverse mood               </li>
                              <li>
                  wayward behavior               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) deviating from what is considered moral or right or proper or good
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/depraved">depraved</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/perverted">perverted</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reprobate">reprobate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>perversion</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : corruption; turning from right to wrong</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a curve that reverses the direction of something
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the tendrils of the plant exhibited perversion               </li>
                              <li>
                  perversion also shows up in kinky telephone cords               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an aberrant sexual practice
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sexual perversion">sexual perversion</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the action of perverting something (turning it to a wrong use)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  it was a perversion of justice               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
