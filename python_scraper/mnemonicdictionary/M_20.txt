      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : change of form; Ex. metamorphosis of caterpillar to butterfly; V. metamorphose: change by metamorphosis</p>

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/metabolism">metabolism</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
