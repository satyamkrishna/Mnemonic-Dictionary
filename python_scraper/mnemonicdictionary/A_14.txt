         (verb) be compatible or in accordance with
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) follow through or carry out a plan without deviation
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  They adhered to their plan               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         (verb) come or be in close contact with; stick or hold together and resist separation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
