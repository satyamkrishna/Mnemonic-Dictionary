      <h2>imperative</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : absolutely necessary; that must be done; critically important; expressing command; Ex. It is imperative that; N: something that must be done</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a mood that expresses an intention to influence the listener's behavior
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imperative form">imperative form</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imperative mood">imperative mood</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jussive mood">jussive mood</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) some duty that is essential and urgent
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) requiring attention or action
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  as nuclear weapons proliferate, preventing war becomes imperative               </li>
                              <li>
                  requests that grew more and more imperative               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) relating to verbs in the imperative mood

      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : like an emperor; related to an empire; CF. imperialism</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a small tufted beard worn by Emperor Napoleon III
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imperial beard">imperial beard</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a piece of luggage carried on top of a coach
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) relating to or associated with an empire
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

         <u>Definition</u><br />

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
