      <h2>buoyant</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) tending to float on a liquid or rise in air or gas
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

      <h2>bureaucracy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : overregulated administrative system marked by red tape; ADJ. bureaucratic</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) nonelective government officials
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bureaucratism">bureaucratism</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a government that is administered primarily by bureaus that are staffed with nonelective officials
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any organization in which action is obstructed by insistence on unnecessary procedures and red tape
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
