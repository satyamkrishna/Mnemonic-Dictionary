      <h2>bombardment</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : attack (as with missiles or bombs); V. bombard</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the rapid and continuous delivery of linguistic communication (spoken or written)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/barrage">barrage</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/onslaught">onslaught</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/outpouring">outpouring</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a barrage of questions               </li>
                              <li>
                  a bombardment of mail complaining about his mistake               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the heavy fire of artillery to saturate an area rather than hit a specific target
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/barrage">barrage</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/barrage fire">barrage fire</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/battery">battery</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shelling">shelling</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  they laid down a barrage in front of the advancing troops               </li>
                              <li>
                  the shelling went on for hours without pausing               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act (or an instance) of subjecting a body or substance to the impact of high-energy particles (as electrons or alpha rays)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an attack by dropping bombs

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bombing">bombing</a>
