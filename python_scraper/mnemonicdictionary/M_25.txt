      <h2>mirage</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unreal reflection; optical illusion</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an optical illusion in which atmospheric refraction by a layer of hot air distorts or inverts reflections of distant objects
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something illusory and unattainable
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>mire</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : entangle; stick in swampy ground; stick or sink in mire; N: bog; deep mud; Ex. sucked deeper into the mire</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a soft wet area of low-lying land that sinks underfoot
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/morass">morass</a>

         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) deep soft mud in water or slush
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
