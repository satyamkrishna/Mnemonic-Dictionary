      <h2>cursive</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of writing) flowing; running; having the successive letters joined</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) rapid handwriting in which letters are set down in full and are cursively connected within words without lifting the writing implement from the paper
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cursive script">cursive script</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/longhand">longhand</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/running hand">running hand</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having successive letter joined together
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  cursive script               </li>

      <h2>curtail</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : shorten; reduce</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) place restrictions on
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/curb">curb</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cut back">cut back</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/restrict">restrict</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  curtail drinking in school               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) terminate or abbreviate before its intended or proper end or its full extent
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clip">clip</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cut short">cut short</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  My speech was cut short               </li>
                              <li>
                  Personal freedom is curtailed in many countries               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>cynosure</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : object of general attention; person or thing that is a center of attention; CF. Ursa Minor</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something that provides guidance (as Polaris guides mariners)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  let faith be your cynosure to walk by               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
