      <h2>delusion</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : false belief; hallucination; deluding; Ex. delusions of grandeur; Ex. under the delusion that</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (psychology) an erroneous belief that is held in the face of evidence to the contrary
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/psychotic belief">psychotic belief</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a mistaken or unfounded opinion or idea
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hallucination">hallucination</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he has delusions of competence               </li>
                              <li>
                  his dreams of vast wealth are a hallucination               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of deluding; deception by creating illusory ideas
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/head game">head game</a>
