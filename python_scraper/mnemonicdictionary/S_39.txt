      <h2>soliloquy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : talking to oneself (esp. in a play); CF. monologue: soliloquy; long speech by one person (often monopolizing a conversation)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) speech you make to yourself
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/monologue">monologue</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a (usually long) dramatic speech intended to give the illusion of unspoken reflections
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
