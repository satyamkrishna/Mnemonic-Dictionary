      <h2>adamant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hard; inflexible</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) very hard native crystalline carbon valued as a gem
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/diamond">diamond</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) impervious to pleas, persuasion, requests, reason
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adamantine">adamantine</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inexorable">inexorable</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/intransigent">intransigent</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>adapt</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make or become suitable for a specific use; alter; modify; adjust; N. adaptation: act of adapting; composition recast into a new form; Ex. The play is an adaption of a short novel.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make fit for, or change to suit a new purpose
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/accommodate">accommodate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Adapt our native cuisine to the available food resources of the new country               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) adapt or conform oneself to new or different conditions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adjust">adjust</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conform">conform</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  We must adjust to the bad economic situation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
