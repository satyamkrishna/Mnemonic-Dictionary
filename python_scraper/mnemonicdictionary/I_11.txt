         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) instrumentation (a piece of equipment or tool) used to effect an end
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) apply in a manner consistent with its purpose or design
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  implement a procedure               </li>
