      <h2>prehensile</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : capable of grasping or holding (esp. by wrapping around); Ex. prehensile tails</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) adapted for grasping especially by wrapping around an object
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a monkey's prehensile tail               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having a keen intellect
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  poets--those gifted strangely prehensile men               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) immoderately desirous of acquiring e.g. wealth
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/avaricious">avaricious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grasping">grasping</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/greedy">greedy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  casting covetous eyes on his neighbor's fields               </li>
