      <h2>torpor</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lethargy; sluggishness; dormancy; ADJ. torpid: lethargic; lazy; inactive; (of an animal) dormant; hibernating</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a state of motor and mental inactivity with a partial suspension of sensibility
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/torpidity">torpidity</a>

            
             
         </div>

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
