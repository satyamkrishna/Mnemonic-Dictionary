      <h2>inept</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unsuited; inappropriate; lacking skill; incompetent; CF. inapt: (of statements or ideas) inappropriate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not elegant or graceful in expression
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/awkward">awkward</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clumsy">clumsy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cumbersome">cumbersome</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ill-chosen">ill-chosen</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inapt">inapt</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an awkward prose style               </li>
                              <li>
                  a clumsy apology               </li>
                              <li>
                  his cumbersome writing style               </li>
                              <li>
                  if the rumor is true, can anything be more inept than to repeat it now?               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) generally incompetent and ineffectual
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/feckless">feckless</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  feckless attempts to repair the plumbing               </li>
                              <li>
                  inept handling of the account               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) revealing lack of perceptiveness or judgment or finesse
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tactless">tactless</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an inept remark               </li>
                              <li>
                  it was tactless to bring up those disagreeable               </li>
                           </ul>
