      <h2>rally</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : come or bring together; call up or summon (forces, vital powers, etc.); revive or recuperate (after illness or difficulty); N: act of rallying; mass gathering</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a large gathering of people intended to arouse enthusiasm
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mass meeting">mass meeting</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the feat of mustering strength for a renewed effort
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rallying">rallying</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  he singled to start a rally in the 9th inning               </li>

                  he feared the rallying of their troops for a counterattack               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
