      <h2>redolent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : odorous; fragrant; suggestive (of an odor); Ex. redolent of onions/mystery</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) serving to bring to mind
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/evocative">evocative</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/remindful">remindful</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reminiscent">reminiscent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resonant">resonant</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  cannot forbear to close on this redolent literary note               </li>
                              <li>
                  a campaign redolent of machine politics               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (used with `of' or `with') noticeably odorous
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/smelling">smelling</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the hall was redolent of floor wax               </li>
                              <li>
                  air redolent with the fumes of beer and whiskey               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having a strong pleasant odor
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aromatic">aromatic</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  the pine woods were more redolent               </li>

      <h2>redoubtable</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : formidable; causing fear</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) inspiring fear
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/formidable">formidable</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unnerving">unnerving</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the formidable prospect of major surgery               </li>
                              <li>
                  a tougher and more redoubtable adversary than the heel-clicking, jackbooted fanatic               </li>
                              <li>
                  something unnerving and prisonlike about high grey wall               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) worthy of respect or honor
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  born of a redoubtable family               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>redundant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : superfluous; more than is necessary; verbose; excessively wordy; repetitious</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) more than is needed, desired, or required
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
