      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lack of caring; indifference; lack of concern or interest in important matters; Ex. He was sunk in apathy after his failure; ADJ. apathetic</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an absence of emotion or enthusiasm

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the trait of lacking enthusiasm for or interest in things generally

      <h2>ape</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : imitate or mimic (a person's behavior or manner)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any of various primates with short tails or no tail at all
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who copies the words or behavior of another
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aper">aper</a>

                        ,

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/emulator">emulator</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imitator">imitator</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) person who resembles a nonhuman primate
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/anthropoid">anthropoid</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) imitate uncritically and in every aspect
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/caricature">caricature</a>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
