      <h2>kaleidoscope</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : tube in which patterns made by the reflection in mirrors of colored pieces of glass, etc. produce interesting symmetrical effects; series of changing events; Ex. kaleidoscope of European history; CF.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a complex pattern of constantly changing colors and shapes
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an optical toy in a tube; it produces symmetrical patterns as bits of colored glass are reflected by mirrors
