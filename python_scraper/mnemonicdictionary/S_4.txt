      <h2>sanguine</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : optimistic; cheerful; hopeful; of the color of blood; red</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a blood-red color
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) confidently optimistic and cheerful
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) inclined to a healthy reddish color often associated with outdoor life
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/florid">florid</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rubicund">rubicund</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ruddy">ruddy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a ruddy complexion               </li>
                              <li>
                  Santa's rubicund cheeks               </li>
                              <li>
                  a fresh and sanguine complexion               </li>
                           </ul>
