      <h2>languor</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lack of physical or mental energy; lassitude; depression</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a relaxed comfortable feeling
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dreaminess">dreaminess</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a feeling of lack of interest or energy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lassitude">lassitude</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/listlessness">listlessness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) inactivity; showing an unusual lack of energy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flatness">flatness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lethargy">lethargy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/phlegm">phlegm</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sluggishness">sluggishness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
