      <h2>acclaim</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : applaud; praise; greet with great approval; announce with great approval; Ex. The new drung has been acclaimed as the most important discoveries for years; N: strong expression of approval and praise</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) enthusiastic approval
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
