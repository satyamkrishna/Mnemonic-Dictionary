      <h2>stipulate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : state as a necessary condition (of an agreement); make express conditions; specify; Ex. He stipulated payment in advance</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) specify as a condition or requirement in a contract or agreement; make an express demand or provision in an agreement
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/condition">condition</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/qualify">qualify</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/specify">specify</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The will stipulates that she can live in the house for the rest of her life               </li>
                              <li>
                  The contract stipulates the dates of the payments               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) give a guarantee or promise of
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  They stipulated to release all the prisoners               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make an oral contract or agreement in the verbal form of question and answer that is necessary to give it legal force
