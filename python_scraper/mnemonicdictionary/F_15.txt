      <h2>fitful</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : spasmodic; intermittent; irregular</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) occurring in spells and often abruptly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spasmodic">spasmodic</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  fitful bursts of energy               </li>
                              <li>
                  spasmodic rifle fire               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/interrupted">interrupted</a>

            <ul>

      <h2>flaccid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : flabby; lacking firmness; weak; Ex. flaccid muscles</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) drooping without elasticity; wanting in stiffness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a flaccid penis               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) out of condition; not strong or robust; incapable of exertion or endurance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flabby">flabby</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/soft">soft</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he was too soft for the army               </li>
                              <li>
                  flabby around the middle               </li>
                              <li>
                  flaccid cheeks               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
