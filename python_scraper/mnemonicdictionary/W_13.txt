      <h2>wreak</h2>

      <h2>wrench</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : twist; pull; strain; Ex. He wrenched the gun out of her hands.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sharp strain on muscles or ligaments
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pull">pull</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twist">twist</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the wrench to his knee occurred as he fell               </li>
                              <li>
                  he was sidelined with a hamstring pull               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a jerky pulling movement
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twist">twist</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a hand tool that is used to hold or twist a nut or bolt
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spanner">spanner</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) twist or pull violently or suddenly, especially so as to remove (something) from that to which it is attached or from where it originates
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twist">twist</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  wrench a window off its hinges               </li>
                              <li>
                  wrench oneself free from somebody's grip               </li>
                              <li>
                  a deep sigh was wrenched from his chest               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make a sudden twisting motion
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) twist and compress, as if in pain or anguish
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/wring">wring</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Wring one's hand               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) twist suddenly so as to sprain
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rick">rick</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sprain">sprain</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/turn">turn</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twist">twist</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/wrick">wrick</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  wrench one's ankle               </li>
                              <li>
                  The wrestler twisted his shoulder               </li>
