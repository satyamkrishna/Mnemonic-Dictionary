      <h2>igneous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : produced by fire; of fire; volcanic; (of rocks) formed from lava; Ex. igneous meteorite</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) produced under conditions involving intense heat
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pyrogenic">pyrogenic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pyrogenous">pyrogenous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  igneous rock is rock formed by solidification from a molten state; especially from molten magma               </li>
                              <li>
                  igneous fusion is fusion by heat alone               </li>
                              <li>
                  pyrogenic strata               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) produced by the action of fire or intense heat
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/eruptive">eruptive</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  rocks formed by igneous agents               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) like or suggestive of fire
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fiery">fiery</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a fiery desert wind               </li>
                              <li>
                  an igneous desert atmosphere               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

              
      <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u><strong>Mnemonics (Memory Aids) for igneous</strong></u> <br /><br />

                  <div class="row-fluid">
            <div class="span9" style="float:left;">
               <i class="icon-lightbulb"></i>   ignite is to fire. "ign" in general as a prefix refers to fire.
