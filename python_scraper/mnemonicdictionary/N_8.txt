      <h2>nondescript</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : undistinctive; ordinary; ordinary-looking; Ex. nondescript fellow in a crowd</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person is not easily classified and not very interesting
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking distinct or individual characteristics; dull and uninteresting
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/characterless">characterless</a>

      <h2>noose</h2>
