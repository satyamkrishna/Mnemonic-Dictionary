      <h2>sagacious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : wise; perceptive; shrewd; having insight; N. sagacity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) acutely insightful and wise
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/perspicacious">perspicacious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sapient">sapient</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  much too perspicacious to be taken in by such a spurious argument               </li>
                              <li>
                  observant and thoughtful, he was given to asking sagacious questions               </li>
                              <li>
                  a source of valuable insights and sapient advice to educators               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) skillful in statecraft or management
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an astute and sagacious statesman               </li>

      <h2>sage</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : person celebrated for wisdom; wise person; ADJ: wise</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a mentor in spiritual and philosophical topics who is renowned for profound wisdom
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) aromatic fresh or dried grey-green leaves used widely as seasoning for meats and fowl and game etc
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any of various plants of the genus Salvia; a cosmopolitan herb
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/salvia">salvia</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having wisdom that comes with age and experience
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of the grey-green color of sage leaves
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sage-green">sage-green</a>

            
             
         </div>
