      <h2>obstreperous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : boisterous; noisy and uncontrollable</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) noisily and stubbornly defiant
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  obstreperous boys               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
