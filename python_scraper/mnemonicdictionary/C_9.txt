      <h2>caricature</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : distortion; burlesque</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a representation of a person that is exaggerated for comic effect
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imitation">imitation</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/impersonation">impersonation</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) represent in or produce a caricature of
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ape">ape</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>carillon</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) set of bells hung in a bell tower
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
