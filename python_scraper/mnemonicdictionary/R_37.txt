      <h2>retroactive</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : taking effect before its enactment (as a law) or imposition (as a tax); (of a law) having effect on the past as well as the future</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) descriptive of any event or stimulus or process that has an effect on the effects of events or stimuli or process that occurred previously
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) affecting things past
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ex post facto">ex post facto</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/retro">retro</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  retroactive tax increase               </li>
                              <li>
                  an ex-post-facto law               </li>
                              <li>
                  retro pay               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>retrograde</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : go backwards; recede; degenerate; deteriorate; ADJ.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move backward in an orbit, of celestial bodies
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move in a direction contrary to the usual one
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  retrograding planets               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move back
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/retreat">retreat</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The glacier retrogrades               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) go back over
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hash over">hash over</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rehash">rehash</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  retrograde arguments               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) get worse or fall back to a previous condition
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/regress">regress</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/retrogress">retrogress</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) moving from east to west on the celestial sphere; or--for planets--around the sun in a direction opposite to that of the Earth
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of amnesia; affecting time immediately preceding trauma
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) going from better to worse
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/retrogressive">retrogressive</a>

            
             
         </div>
