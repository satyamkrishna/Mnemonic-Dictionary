      <h2>dorsal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : relating to the back of an animal; Ex. dorsal fin</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) belonging to or on or near the back or upper surface of an animal or organ or part
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the dorsal fin is the vertical fin on the back of a fish and certain marine mammals               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) facing away from the axis of an organ or organism
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abaxial">abaxial</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the abaxial surface of a leaf is the underside or side facing away from the stem               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
