         (noun) goods whose importation or exportation or possession is prohibited by law
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>contravene</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : contradict; oppose; violate (a rule, law, or custom); N. contravention</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) go against, as of rules and laws
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conflict">conflict</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/infringe">infringe</a>

                        ,

         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>contrived</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unnatural and forced; artificial; not spontaneous; Ex. The ending was rather contrived.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) showing effects of planning or manipulation
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a novel with a contrived ending               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) artificially formal
