      <h2>blatant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : extremely (offensively) obvious; loudly offensive; Ex. blatant lie; N. blatancy</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) without any attempt at concealment; completely obvious
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/blazing">blazing</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conspicuous">conspicuous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  blatant disregard of the law               </li>
                              <li>
                  a blatant appeal to vanity               </li>
                              <li>
                  a blazing indiscretion               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) conspicuously and offensively loud; given to vehement outcry
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clamant">clamant</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/strident">strident</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vociferous">vociferous</a>

      <h2>bleak</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : cold or cheerless; frigid; unlikely to be favorable; depressing</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) offering little or no hope
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/black">black</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dim">dim</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the future looked black               </li>
                              <li>
                  prospects were bleak               </li>
                              <li>
                  Life in the Aran Islands has always been bleak and difficult               </li>
                              <li>
                  took a dim view of things               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) providing no shelter or sustenance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bare">bare</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/barren">barren</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/desolate">desolate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stark">stark</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  bare rocky hills               </li>
                              <li>
                  barren lands               </li>
                              <li>
                  the bleak treeless regions of the high Andes               </li>
                              <li>
                  the desolate surface of the moon               </li>
                              <li>
                  a stark landscape               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) unpleasantly cold and damp
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cutting">cutting</a>
