      <h2>hegemony</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : dominance especially of one nation over others</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the dominance or leadership of one social group or nation over others
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the hegemony of a single member state is not incompatible with a genuine confederation               </li>
                              <li>
                  to say they have priority is not to say they have complete hegemony               </li>
                              <li>
                  the consolidation of the United States' hegemony over a new international economic system               </li>
