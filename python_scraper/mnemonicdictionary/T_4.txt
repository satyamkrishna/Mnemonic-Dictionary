      <h2>tarnish</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make or become dull or discolored; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) discoloration of metal surface caused by oxidation
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make dirty or spotty, as by exposure to air; also used metaphorically
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/defile">defile</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/maculate">maculate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stain">stain</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sully">sully</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The silver was tarnished by the long exposure to the air               </li>
                              <li>
                  Her reputation was sullied after the affair with a married man               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
