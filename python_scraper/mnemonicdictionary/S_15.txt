      <h2>seduce</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lead away from proper conduct; entice; ADJ. seductive</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) induce to have sex
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/make">make</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/score">score</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  Harry finally seduced Sally               </li>
                              <li>
                  Did you score last night?               </li>
                              <li>
                  Harry made Sally               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) lure or entice away from duty, principles, or proper conduct
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
