      <h2>prerogative</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : privilege; unquestionable right; CF. ask before</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a right reserved exclusively by a particular person or group (especially a hereditary or official right)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/exclusive right">exclusive right</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/perquisite">perquisite</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/privilege">privilege</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>presage</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : foretell; be a warning or sign of; N: presentiment; foreboding; omen</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a foreboding about what is about to happen
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sign of something about to happen
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/omen">omen</a>

         </div>
