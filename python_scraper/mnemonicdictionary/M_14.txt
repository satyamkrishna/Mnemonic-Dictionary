      <h2>maverick</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : rebel; nonconformist (in a group)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who exhibits great independence in thought and action
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rebel">rebel</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unbranded range animal (especially a stray calf); belongs to the first person who puts a brand on it
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) independent in behavior or thought

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/irregular">irregular</a>
