         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a junction where two things (as paper or film or magnetic tape) have been joined together

      <h2>spoonerism</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : accidental transposition of sounds in successive words; Ex. ``Let me sew you to your sheet'' for ``Let me show you to your seat''; CF. William Spooner</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) transposition of initial consonants in a pair of words
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
