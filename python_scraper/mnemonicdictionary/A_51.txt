      <h2>ascetic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : practicing self-denial; avoiding physical pleasures and comforts; austere; Ex. ascetic life of Buddhist monks; N. asceticism</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who practices self denial as a spiritual discipline
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abstainer">abstainer</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) pertaining to or characteristic of an ascetic or the practice of rigorous self-discipline
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ascetical">ascetical</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  ascetic practices               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) practicing great self-denial
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ascetical">ascetical</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/austere">austere</a>
