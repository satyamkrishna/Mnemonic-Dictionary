      <h2>alchemy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : medieval chemistry</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the way two individuals relate to each other
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/chemistry">chemistry</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/interpersonal chemistry">interpersonal chemistry</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>alias</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : an assumed name esp. by a criminal (usually to mislead people); ADV. alias</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a name that has been assumed temporarily
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

         </div>

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/a.k.a.">a.k.a.</a>
