         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by a lightly pert and exuberant quality

      <h2>pertinent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : suitable; to the point; relevant</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having precise or logical relevance to the matter at hand
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a list of articles pertinent to the discussion               </li>
                              <li>
                  remarks that were to the point               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being of striking appropriateness and pertinence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/apposite">apposite</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/apt">apt</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the successful copywriter is a master of apposite and evocative verbal images               </li>
                              <li>
                  an apt reply               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
