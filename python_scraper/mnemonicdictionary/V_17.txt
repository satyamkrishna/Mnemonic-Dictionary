      <h2>visionary</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : produced by imagination; fanciful; mystical; showing foresight; N: one having foresight; one given to speculative impractical ideas</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person given to fanciful speculations and enthusiasms with little regard for what is actually possible
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person with unusual powers of foresight
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/illusionist">illusionist</a>

         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not practical or realizable; speculative

            <u>Synonyms</u> :

      <h2>vituperative</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : abusive; scolding; V. vituperate: berate; scold; rail against abusively</p>
