      <h2>downcast</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : disheartened; dejected; sad; directed downward</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a ventilation shaft through which air enters a mine
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) directed downward
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  a downcast glance               </li>
