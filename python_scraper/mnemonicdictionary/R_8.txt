         (noun) (usually plural) a destructive action

      <h2>rave</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : overwhelmingly favorable review; V: talk wildly as if mad; Ex. raving lunatic</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a dance party that lasts all night and electronically synthesized music is played
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  raves are very popular in Berlin               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an extravagantly enthusiastic review
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he gave it a rave               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) participate in an all-night techno dance party
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) talk in a noisy, excited, or declamatory manner
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jabber">jabber</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spout">spout</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) praise enthusiastically
