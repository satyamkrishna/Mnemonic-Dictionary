         (adj) elaborately or excessively ornamented

      <h2>flounder</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : struggle and thrash about; proceed clumsily or falter (as in water, mud, snow, etc.); proceed in confusion</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) flesh of any of various American and European flatfish
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any of various European and non-European marine flatfish
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) walk with great difficulty
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stagger">stagger</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He staggered along in the heavy snow               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) behave awkwardly; have difficulties
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She is floundering in college               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
