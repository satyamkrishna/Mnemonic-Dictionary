      <h2>fractious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unruly; peevish; cranky; bad-tempered; Ex. fractious horse</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) stubbornly resistant to authority or control
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/recalcitrant">recalcitrant</a>

      <h2>frail</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : weak; N. frailty</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the weight of a frail (basket) full of raisins or figs; between 50 and 75 pounds
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a basket for holding dried fruit (especially raisins or figs)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) physically weak
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an invalid's frail body               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) wanting in moral strength, courage, or will; having the attributes of man as opposed to e.g. divine beings
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fallible">fallible</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imperfect">imperfect</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/weak">weak</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I'm only a fallible human               </li>
                              <li>
                  frail humanity               </li>
                           </ul>
