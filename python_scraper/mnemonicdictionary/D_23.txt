      <h2>determinate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : having a fixed order of procedure; precisely defined; invariable; fixed; conclusive; final</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) precisely determined or limited or defined; especially fixed by rule or by a specific and constant cause
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a determinate distance               </li>
                              <li>

                              <li>
                  determinate variations in animals               </li>
                           </ul>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not continuing to grow indefinitely at the apex
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  determinate growth               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
