      <h2>peroration</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : conclusion of an oration; perorating; V. perorate: conclude a speech; speak at great length</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a flowery and highly rhetorical oration
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (rhetoric) the concluding section of an oration
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he summarized his main points in his peroration               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
