      <h2>meteoric</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : like a meteor; swift; momentarily brilliant; Ex. meteoric rise to fame; N. meteor</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or pertaining to atmospheric phenomena, especially weather and weather conditions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/meteorologic">meteorologic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/meteorological">meteorological</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  meteorological factors               </li>
                              <li>
                  meteorological chart               </li>
                              <li>
                  meteoric (or meteorological) phenomena               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) pertaining to or consisting of meteors or meteoroids
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  meteoric shower               </li>
                              <li>
                  meteoric impacts               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) like a meteor in speed or brilliance or transience
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>meter</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : arrangement of words in the form of poetry (by accentual rhythm)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the basic unit of length adopted under the Systeme International d'Unites (approximately 1.094 yards)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/m">m</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/metre">metre</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any of various measuring instruments for measuring a quantity
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (prosody) the accent in a metrical foot of verse
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/beat">beat</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cadence">cadence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/measure">measure</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/metre">metre</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) rhythm as given by division into parts of equal duration
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/metre">metre</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/time">time</a>
