      <h2>servile</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : slavish; cringing; N. servility</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) submissive or fawning in attitude or behavior
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spoke in a servile tone               </li>
                              <li>
                  the incurably servile housekeeper               </li>
                              <li>
                  servile tasks such as floor scrubbing and barn work               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) relating to or involving slaves or appropriate for slaves or servants
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Brown's attempt at servile insurrection               </li>
                              <li>
                  the servile wars of Sicily               </li>
                              <li>
                  servile work               </li>
                           </ul>
