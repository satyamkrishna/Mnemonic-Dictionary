      <h2>chore</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : daily domestic task (such as cleaning, cooking, and shopping); unpleasant task</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a specific piece of work required to be done as a duty or for a specific fee
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/job">job</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/task">task</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>choreography</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : art of representing dances in written symbols; arrangement of dances</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a show involving artistic dancing
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stage dancing">stage dancing</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the representation of dancing by symbols as music is represented by notes
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a notation used by choreographers

      <h2>chorus</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : phrase repeated throughout a song or poem</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any utterance produced simultaneously by a group
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a chorus of boos               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a group of people assembled to sing together
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the part of a song where a soloist is joined by a group of singers
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/refrain">refrain</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a body of dancers or singers who perform together
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/chorus line">chorus line</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a company of actors who comment (by speaking or singing in unison) on the action in a classical Greek play
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/greek chorus">greek chorus</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) utter in unison
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  `yes,' the children chorused               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) sing in a choir
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/choir">choir</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>chronic</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
