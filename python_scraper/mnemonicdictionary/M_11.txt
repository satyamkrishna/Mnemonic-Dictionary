      <h2>martial</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : warlike; of war; Ex. martial art/law</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) Roman poet noted for epigrams (first century BC)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (of persons) befitting a warrior
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/soldierlike">soldierlike</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/soldierly">soldierly</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/warriorlike">warriorlike</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a military bearing               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) suggesting war or military life
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/warlike">warlike</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to the armed forces
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
