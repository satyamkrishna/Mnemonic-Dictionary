      <h2>academic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : related to a school; not practical or directly useful</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an educator who works at a college or university
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/academician">academician</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/faculty member">faculty member</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) associated with academia or an academy
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the academic curriculum               </li>
                              <li>
                  academic gowns               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) hypothetical or theoretical and not expected to produce an immediate or practical result
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>

                  an academic question               </li>
