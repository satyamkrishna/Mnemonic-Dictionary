         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) malicious satisfaction
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) dwell on with satisfaction
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/crow">crow</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/triumph">triumph</a>

      <h2>gloss</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : brief explanation note or translation of a difficult expression; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an explanation or definition of an obscure word in a text
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rubric">rubric</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an alphabetical list of technical terms in some specialized field of knowledge; usually published as an appendix to a text on that field
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/glossary">glossary</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the property of being smooth and shiny
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/burnish">burnish</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/glossiness">glossiness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/polish">polish</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an outward or token appearance or form that is deliberately misleading
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/color">color</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/colour">colour</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/semblance">semblance</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  he hoped his claims would have a semblance of authenticity               </li>

                  he tried to give his falsehood the gloss of moral sanction               </li>
                              <li>

                           </ul>

         (verb) give a shine or gloss to, usually by rubbing
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) provide interlinear explanations for words or phrases
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
