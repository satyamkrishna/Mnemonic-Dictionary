      <h2>ossify</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : change or harden into bone; become hard and unchanging in one's ideas</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) become bony
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The tissue ossified               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make rigid and set into a conventional pattern
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/petrify">petrify</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rigidify">rigidify</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  rigidify the training schedule               </li>
                              <li>
                  ossified teaching methods               </li>
                              <li>
                  slogans petrify our thinking               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to become hard and bony
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

      <h2>ostensible</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : apparent; appearing as such; professed(pretended); pretended; Ex. ostensible purpose of the expedition</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) appearing as such but not necessarily so
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/apparent">apparent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/seeming">seeming</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  for all his apparent wealth he had no money to pay the rent               </li>
                              <li>
                  the committee investigated some apparent discrepancies               </li>
                              <li>
                  the ostensible truth of their theories               </li>
                              <li>
                  his seeming honesty               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) represented or appearing as such; pretended
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ostensive">ostensive</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  His ostensible purpose was charity, his real goal popularity               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
