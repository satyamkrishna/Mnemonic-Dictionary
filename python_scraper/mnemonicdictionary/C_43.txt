      <h2>compound</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : combine; produce by combining; increase; make worse by adding to or increasing; exacerbate; Ex. compound an error; ADJ: consisting of two or more parts; N: combination of two or more parts; area enclo</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a whole formed by a union of two or more elements or parts
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (chemistry) a substance formed by chemical union of two or more elements or ingredients in definite proportion by weight
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/chemical compound">chemical compound</a>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an enclosure of residences and other building (especially in the Orient)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make more intense, stronger, or more marked

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
