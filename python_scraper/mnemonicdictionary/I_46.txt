      <h2>intervene</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : come between; interfere; Ex. intervened to prevent a fight; N. intervention</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) get involved, so as to alter or hinder an action, or through force or threat of force
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/interfere">interfere</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/interpose">interpose</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/step in">step in</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Why did the U.S. not intervene earlier in WW II?               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) be placed or located between other things or extend between spaces and events
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  This interludes intervenes between the two movements               </li>
                              <li>
                  Eight days intervened               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) occur between other event or between certain points of time
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the war intervened between the birth of her two children               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

      <h2>intimate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hint; suggest; imply; ADJ: marked by close relationship; familiar; private; personal; Ex. intimate knowledge/thoughts in the diary; N: close friend or confidant; CF. intimacy</p>

         (noun) someone to whom private matters are confided
