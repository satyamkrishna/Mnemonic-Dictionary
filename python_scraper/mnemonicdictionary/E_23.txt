      <h2>epic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : long heroic poem, novel, or similar work of art (celebrating the feats of a hero); ADJ: (of stories or events) resembling an epic; grand</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a long narrative poem telling of a hero's deeds
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/epic poem">epic poem</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/epos">epos</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/heroic poem">heroic poem</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) very imposing or impressive; surpassing the ordinary (especially in size or scale)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/heroic">heroic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/larger-than-life">larger-than-life</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>episodic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of a story or play) loosely connected; made up of separate and loosely connected parts; N. episode: incident in the course of an experience</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of writing or narration; divided into or composed of episodes
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the book is episodic and the incidents don't always hang together               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) occurring or appearing at usually irregular intervals
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/occasional">occasional</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  episodic in his affections               </li>
                              <li>
                  occasional headaches               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) limited in duration to a single episode
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an account concerned primarily with episodic events such as the succession of rulers               </li>
                           </ul>
