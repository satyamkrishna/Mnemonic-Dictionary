      <h2>lurid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : wild; sensational; graphic; gruesome; horrible; Ex. lurid details of the murder</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) horrible in fierceness or savagery
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  lurid crimes               </li>
                              <li>
                  a lurid life               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) glaringly vivid and graphic; marked by sensationalism
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shocking">shocking</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  lurid details of the accident               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) shining with an unnatural red glow as of fire seen through smoke
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a lurid sunset               </li>
                              <li>
                  lurid flames               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) ghastly pale
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  moonlight gave the statue a lurid luminence               </li>
                           </ul>

         </div>
