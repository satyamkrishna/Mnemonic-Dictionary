      <h2>antagonism</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hostility; active opposition; V. antagonize: cause to become an enemy; N. antagonist: person who is opposed to another; opponent; adversary; principal character in oppostion to the protagonist</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a state of deep-seated ill-will
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/enmity">enmity</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hostility">hostility</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the relation between opposing principles or forces or factors
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the inherent antagonism of capitalism and socialism               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an actively expressed feeling of dislike and hostility
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (biochemistry) interference in or inhibition of the physiological action of a chemical substance by another having a similar structure
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>antediluvian</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : antiquated; extremely ancient</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any of the early patriarchs who lived prior to the Noachian deluge
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/antediluvian patriarch">antediluvian patriarch</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a very old person
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ancient">ancient</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to the period before the biblical flood
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/antediluvial">antediluvial</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  antediluvian man               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) so extremely old as seeming to belong to an earlier period
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/antiquated">antiquated</a>
