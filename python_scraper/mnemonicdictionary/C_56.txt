      <h2>constituent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : supporter; voter; member of a constituency; component</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an artifact that is one of the individual parts of which a composite entity is made up; especially a part that can be separated from or attached to a system
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/component">component</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/element">element</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spare components for cars               </li>
                              <li>
                  a component or constituent element of a system               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a member of a constituency; a citizen who is represented in a government by officials for whom he or she votes
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  needs continued support by constituents to be re-elected               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something determined in relation to something that includes it
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
