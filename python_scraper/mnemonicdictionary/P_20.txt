      <h2>perennial</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : something long-lasting; perennial plant; ADJ: lasting through the year or many years; lasting for a long time; enduring</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (botany) a plant lasting for three seasons or more
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lasting three seasons or more
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the common buttercup is a popular perennial plant               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lasting an indefinitely long time; suggesting self-renewal
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  perennial happiness               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) recurring again and again
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/recurrent">recurrent</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/repeated">repeated</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>perfidious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : treacherous; disloyal; N. perfidy: treachery</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) tending to betray; especially having a treacherous character as attributed to the Carthaginians by the Romans
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/punic">punic</a>

         </div>
