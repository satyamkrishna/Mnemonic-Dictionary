      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a purging medicine; stimulates evacuation of the bowels
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aperient">aperient</a>

         (adj) emotionally purging
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/psychotherapeutic">psychotherapeutic</a>
