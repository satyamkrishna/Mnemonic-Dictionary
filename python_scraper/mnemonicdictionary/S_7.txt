      <h2>scaffold</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : temporary platform for workers (to work at heights above the ground); bracing framework; platform for execution</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a platform from which criminals are executed (hanged or beheaded)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a temporary arrangement erected around a building for convenience of workers
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) provide with a scaffold for support
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
