      <h2>sentient</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : capable of sensation and conscious; aware; sensitive; Ex. sentient creature; N. sentience</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) endowed with feeling and unstructured consciousness
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/animate">animate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the living knew themselves just sentient puppets on God's stage               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) consciously perceiving
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  sentient of the intolerable load               </li>
                              <li>
                  a boy so sentient of his surroundings               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

               <i class="icon-lightbulb"></i>   Sentimental people are sentinent.
