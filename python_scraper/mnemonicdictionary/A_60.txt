      <h2>autocratic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : having absolute unchecked power; dictatorial; N. autocrat, autocracy</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) offensively self-assured or given to exercising usually unwarranted power
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bossy">bossy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dominating">dominating</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/high-and-mighty">high-and-mighty</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/magisterial">magisterial</a>

         </div>
