      <h2>thesis</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : opinion put forward and supported by reasoned arguments</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unproved statement put forward as a premise in an argument
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
