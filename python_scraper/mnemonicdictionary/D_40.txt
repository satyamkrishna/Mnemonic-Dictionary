      <h2>disputatious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : argumentative; fond of argument</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) inclined or showing an inclination to dispute or disagree, even to engage in law suits
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/combative">combative</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/contentious">contentious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disputative">disputative</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/litigious">litigious</a>

            
             
         </div>
