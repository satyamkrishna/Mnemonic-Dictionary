         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) do something that one considers to be below one's dignity
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deign">deign</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) debase oneself morally, act in an undignified, unworthy, or dishonorable way
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lower oneself">lower oneself</a>
