      <h2>rigid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hard and unbending; stiff and unyielding; fixed in behavior or views; strict; rigorous; Ex. rigid rule</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) incapable of or resistant to bending
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stiff">stiff</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a rigid strip of metal               </li>
                              <li>
                  a table made of rigid plastic               </li>
                              <li>
                  a palace guardsman stiff as a poker               </li>
                              <li>
                  stiff hair               </li>
                              <li>
                  a stiff neck               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) incapable of compromise or flexibility
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/strict">strict</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) incapable of adapting or changing to meet circumstances
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inflexible">inflexible</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unbending">unbending</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a rigid disciplinarian               </li>
                              <li>
                  an inflexible law               </li>
                              <li>
                  an unbending will to dominate               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

         <u>Definition</u><br />
