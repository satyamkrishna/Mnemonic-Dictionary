      <h2>bargain</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : agreement between two groups or people; something for sale at a price advantageous to the buyer; V: negotiate; trade; Ex. bargaining power</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an agreement between parties (usually arrived at after discussion) fixing obligations of each
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deal">deal</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he made a bargain with the devil               </li>
                              <li>
                  he rose to prominence through a series of shady deals               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an advantageous purchase
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/buy">buy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/steal">steal</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  she got a bargain at the auction               </li>
                              <li>
                  the stock was a real buy at that price               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) negotiate the terms of an exchange
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dicker">dicker</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  We bargained for a beautiful rug in the bazaar               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>baroque</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : highly ornate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the historic period from about 1600 until 1750 when the baroque style of art, architecture, and music flourished in Europe
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/baroque era">baroque era</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/baroque period">baroque period</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) elaborate and extensive ornamentation in decorative art and architecture that flourished in Europe in the 17th century
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/baroqueness">baroqueness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having elaborate symmetrical ornamentation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/churrigueresco">churrigueresco</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/churrigueresque">churrigueresque</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the building...frantically baroque               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to or characteristic of the elaborately ornamented style of architecture, art, and music popular in Europe between 1600 and 1750
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
