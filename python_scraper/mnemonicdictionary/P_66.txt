      <h2>provincial</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pertaining to a province; limited in outlook; narrow; unsophisticated</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (Roman Catholic Church) an official in charge of an ecclesiastical province acting under the superior general of a religious order
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the general of the Jesuits receives monthly reports from the provincials               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a country person
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bucolic">bucolic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/peasant">peasant</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or associated with a province
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  provincial government               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characteristic of the provinces or their people
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
