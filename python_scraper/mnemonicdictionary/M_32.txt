      <h2>monotony</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sameness leading to boredom; monotonousness; ADJ. monotonous; CF. monotone</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the quality of wearisome constancy, routine, and lack of variety
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/humdrum">humdrum</a>

         </div>

                  he hated the sameness of the food the college served               </li>
                           </ul>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
