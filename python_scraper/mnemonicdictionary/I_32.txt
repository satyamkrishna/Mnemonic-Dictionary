      <h2>inevitable</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unavoidable</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unavoidable event
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  don't argue with the inevitable               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) incapable of being avoided or prevented
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the inevitable result               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) invariably occurring or appearing
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

      <h2>inexorable</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : relentless; unyielding; implacable; not capable of being changed by entreaty or efforts; Ex. inexorable price rises</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not to be placated or appeased or moved by entreaty
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grim">grim</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/relentless">relentless</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stern">stern</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unappeasable">unappeasable</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unforgiving">unforgiving</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unrelenting">unrelenting</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  grim determination               </li>
                              <li>
                  grim necessity               </li>
                              <li>
                  Russia's final hour, it seemed, approached with inexorable certainty               </li>
                              <li>
                  relentless persecution               </li>
                              <li>
                  the stern demands of parenthood               </li>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adamant">adamant</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/intransigent">intransigent</a>

            
             
         </div>
