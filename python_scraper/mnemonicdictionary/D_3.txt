      <h2>daze</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : stun as with a blow or shock; bemuse; benumb; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the feeling of distress and disbelief that you have when something bad happens accidentally
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shock">shock</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stupor">stupor</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his mother's death left him in a daze               </li>
                              <li>
                  he was numb with shock               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) confusion characterized by lack of clarity
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fog">fog</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/haze">haze</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) to cause someone to lose clear vision, especially from intense light
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bedazzle">bedazzle</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dazzle">dazzle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She was dazzled by the bright headlights               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) overcome as with astonishment or disbelief
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bedaze">bedaze</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stun">stun</a>

            
             
         </div>
