      <h2>prodigious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : enormous; marvelous; extraordinary; Ex. prodigious amount/memory</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) so great in size or force or extent as to elicit awe
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/colossal">colossal</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stupendous">stupendous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  colossal crumbling ruins of an ancient temple               </li>
                              <li>
                  has a colossal nerve               </li>
                              <li>
                  a prodigious storm               </li>
                              <li>
                  a stupendous field of grass               </li>
                              <li>
                  stupendous demand               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of momentous or ominous significance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/portentous">portentous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  such a portentous...monster raised all my curiosity               </li>
                              <li>
                  a prodigious vision               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) far beyond what is usual in magnitude or degree
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/exceeding">exceeding</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/exceptional">exceptional</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/surpassing">surpassing</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  an exceptional memory               </li>

      <h2>prodigy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : highly gifted child; person with exceptional talents; marvel; wonder</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unusually gifted or intelligent (young) person; someone whose talents excite wonder and admiration
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  she is a chess prodigy               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sign of something about to happen
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/omen">omen</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/portent">portent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/presage">presage</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prognostic">prognostic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prognostication">prognostication</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he looked for an omen before going into battle               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an impressive or wonderful example of a particular quality
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the Marines are expected to perform prodigies of valor               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

              
      <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u><strong>Mnemonics (Memory Aids) for prodigy</strong></u> <br /><br />

      <h2>profound</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : deep; not superficial; complete; Ex. profound thinker/remark/silence/deafness; N. profundity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) showing intellectual penetration or emotional depth
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
