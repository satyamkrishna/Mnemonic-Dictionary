      <h2>lustrous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : shining; brilliant; Ex. lustrous hair</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) made smooth and bright by or as if by rubbing; reflecting a sheen or glow
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bright">bright</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/burnished">burnished</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shining">shining</a>

                        ,

         </div>

                  bright silver candlesticks               </li>

                  a burnished brass knocker               </li>

                  she brushed her hair until it fell in lustrous auburn waves               </li>

                  rows of shining glasses               </li>

      <h2>luxuriant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : abundant; growing healthily and in large amounts; excessively ornate; rich and splendid; fertile; Ex. luxuriant forests/prose; CF. luxurious, luxuriate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by complexity and richness of detail
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/elaborate">elaborate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                              <li>
                  an elaborate lace pattern               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) displaying luxury and furnishing gratification to the senses
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/epicurean">epicurean</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/luxurious">luxurious</a>
