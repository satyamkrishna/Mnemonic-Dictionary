      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : choosing at random; confused; not based on careful distinctions</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) failing to make or recognize distinctions
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not marked by fine distinctions
