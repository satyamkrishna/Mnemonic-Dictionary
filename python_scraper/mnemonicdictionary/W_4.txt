      <h2>warrant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : justification; written order that serves as authorization (esp. a judicial writ); Ex. search/death warrant; V: justify; guarantee</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a writ from a court commanding police to perform specified acts
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a type of security issued by a corporation (usually together with a bond or preferred stock) that gives the holder the right to purchase a certain amount of common stock at a stated price
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stock warrant">stock warrant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stock-purchase warrant">stock-purchase warrant</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  as a sweetener they offered warrants along with the fixed-income securities               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) formal and explicit approval
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/countenance">countenance</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/endorsement">endorsement</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imprimatur">imprimatur</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/indorsement">indorsement</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sanction">sanction</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a Democrat usually gets the union's endorsement               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a written assurance that some product or service will be provided or will meet certain specifications
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/guarantee">guarantee</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/warrantee">warrantee</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/warranty">warranty</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) show to be reasonable or provide adequate ground for
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/justify">justify</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The emergency does not warrant all of us buying guns               </li>
                              <li>
                  The end justifies the means               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) stand behind and guarantee the quality, accuracy, or condition of
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/guarantee">guarantee</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
