      <h2>menace</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : threat; V: threaten</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something that is a source of danger
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/threat">threat</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  earthquakes are a constant threat in Japan               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a threat or the act of threatening
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he spoke with desperate menace               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) pose a threat to; present a danger to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/endanger">endanger</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imperil">imperil</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jeopardise">jeopardise</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jeopardize">jeopardize</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/peril">peril</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/threaten">threaten</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The pollution is endangering the crops               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) express a threat either by an utterance or a gesture
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he menaced the bank manager with a stick               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) act in a threatening manner
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  A menacing person               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
