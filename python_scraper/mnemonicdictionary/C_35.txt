         (noun) a flat metal piece (usually a disc) used as money

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make up

      <h2>coincidence</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : the chance occurrence, at the same time, of two or more seemingly connected events; V. coincide: happen at the same time; be in agreement; CF. coincident; CF. coincidental</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an event that might have been arranged although it was really accidental
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/happenstance">happenstance</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the quality of occupying the same position or area in space
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he waited for the coincidence of the target and the cross hairs               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the temporal property of two things happening at the same time
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/co-occurrence">co-occurrence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/concurrence">concurrence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conjunction">conjunction</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
