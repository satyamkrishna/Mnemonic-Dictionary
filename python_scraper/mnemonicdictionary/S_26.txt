      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to the stars or constellations
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>

                           </ul>
         </div>

      <h2>sift</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : put through a sieve to separate fine from coarse particles; sort or examine carefully</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move as if through a sieve
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The soldiers sifted through the woods               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) separate by passing through a sieve or other straining device to separate out coarser elements
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sieve">sieve</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/strain">strain</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  sift the flour               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) check and sort carefully
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
