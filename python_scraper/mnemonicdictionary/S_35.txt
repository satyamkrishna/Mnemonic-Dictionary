      <h2>smear</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : spread or cover with a sticky substance; N: mark made by smearing</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) slanderous defamation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/malignment">malignment</a>

         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a thin tissue or blood sample spread on a glass slide and stained for cytologic examination and diagnosis under a microscope
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cytologic smear">cytologic smear</a>
