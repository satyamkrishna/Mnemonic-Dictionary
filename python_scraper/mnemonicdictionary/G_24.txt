      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) naive and easily deceived or tricked
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fleeceable">fleeceable</a>

         </div>

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) easily tricked because of being too trusting
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>gush</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of liquid) pour out in large quantities from a hole; make an excessive display of feeling (without true feeling); Ex. Blood gushed from the wound.</p>
