      <h2>tangible</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : able to be touched; real; concrete; palpable; possible to realize or understand; Ex. tangible proof</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) perceptible by the senses especially the sense of touch
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/touchable">touchable</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  skin with a tangible roughness               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) capable of being treated as fact
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/real">real</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  tangible evidence               </li>
                              <li>
                  his brief time as Prime Minister brought few real benefits to the poor               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (of especially business assets) having physical substance and intrinsic monetary value
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  tangible property like real estate               </li>
                              <li>
                  tangible assets such as machinery               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) capable of being perceived; especially capable of being handled or touched or felt
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/palpable">palpable</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a barely palpable dust               </li>
                              <li>
                  felt sudden anger in a palpable wave               </li>
                              <li>
                  the air was warm and close--palpable as cotton               </li>
                              <li>
                  a palpable lie               </li>
                           </ul>
