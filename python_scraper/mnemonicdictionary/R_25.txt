      <h2>reparation</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : compensation (for loss or wrong); amends; Ex. make reparation for the damage; CF. repair</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) compensation (given or received) for an insult or injury
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an act for which there is no reparation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (usually plural) compensation exacted from a defeated nation by the victors
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Germany was unable to pay the reparations demanded after World War I               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of putting something in working order again
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fix">fix</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/repair">repair</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something done or paid in expiation of a wrong
