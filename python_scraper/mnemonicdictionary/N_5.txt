      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (adj) lower
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>nettle</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : irritate; annoy; vex; ADJ. nettlesome</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any of numerous plants having stinging hairs that cause skin irritation on contact (especially of the genus Urtica or family Urticaceae)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) sting with or as with nettles and cause a stinging pain or sensation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/urticate">urticate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
