      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) inclined to a healthy reddish color often associated with outdoor life
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/florid">florid</a>

      <h2>rudimentary</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : elementary; not developed; crude; N. rudiment: fundamental element or principle; Ex. rudiments of the language</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being or involving basic facts or principles
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fundamental">fundamental</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/underlying">underlying</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the fundamental laws of the universe               </li>
                              <li>
                  a fundamental incomatibility between them               </li>
                              <li>
                  these rudimentary truths               </li>
                              <li>
                  underlying principles               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being in the earliest stages of development
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  rudimentary plans               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not fully developed in mature animals
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vestigial">vestigial</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  rudimentary wings               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
