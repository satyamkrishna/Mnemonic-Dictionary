      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : club; heavy-headed weapon; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) a club used as a weapon
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) overcome or coerce as if by using a heavy club
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
