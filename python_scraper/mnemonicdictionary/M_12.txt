      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a party of guests wearing costumes and masks

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fancy dress">fancy dress</a>
