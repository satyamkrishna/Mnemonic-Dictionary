      <h2>vernacular</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : living language (as compared to the official language); language spoken in a country or region; natural style; Ex. lapse into the vernacular</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a characteristic language of a particular group (as among thieves)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/argot">argot</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cant">cant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jargon">jargon</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lingo">lingo</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/patois">patois</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/slang">slang</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  they don't speak our lingo               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the everyday speech of the people (as distinguished from literary language)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being or characteristic of or appropriate to everyday language
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/common">common</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vulgar">vulgar</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  common parlance               </li>
                              <li>
                  a vernacular term               </li>
                              <li>
                  vernacular speakers               </li>
                              <li>
                  the vulgar tongue of the masses               </li>
                              <li>
                  the technical and vulgar names for an animal species               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>vernal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pertaining to spring</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) suggestive of youth; vigorous and fresh
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/young">young</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/youthful">youthful</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he is young for his age               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or characteristic of or occurring in spring
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the vernal equinox               </li>
