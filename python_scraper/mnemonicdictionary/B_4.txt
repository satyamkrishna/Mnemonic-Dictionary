      <h2>bank</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : heap; piled-up mass; embankment; lateral tilting (as of an aircraft in turning); V: pile up; protect with a bank; tilt in turning</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) sloping land (especially the slope beside a body of water)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  they pulled the canoe up on the bank               </li>
                              <li>
                  he sat on the bank of the river and watched the currents               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a financial institution that accepts deposits and channels the money into lending activities
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/banking company">banking company</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/banking concern">banking concern</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/depository financial institution">depository financial institution</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he cashed a check at the bank               </li>
                              <li>
                  that bank holds the mortgage on my home               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a long ridge or pile
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a huge bank of earth               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an arrangement of similar objects in a row or in tiers
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he operated a bank of switches               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a supply or stock held in reserve for future use (especially in emergencies)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the funds held by a gambling house or the dealer in some gambling games
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he tried to break the bank at Monte Carlo               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a slope in the turn of a road or track; the outside is higher than the inside in order to reduce the effects of centrifugal force
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/camber">camber</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cant">cant</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a container (usually with a slot in the top) for keeping money at home
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/coin bank">coin bank</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/money box">money box</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/savings bank">savings bank</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the coin bank was empty               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a building in which the business of banking transacted
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bank building">bank building</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the bank is on the corner of Nassau and Witherspoon               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a flight maneuver; aircraft tips laterally about its longitudinal axis (especially in turning)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the plane went into a steep bank               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) tip laterally
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the pilot had to bank the aircraft               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) enclose with a bank
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) do business with a bank or keep an account at a bank
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>barefaced</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : shameless and noticeable; blatant; bold; unconcealed; having no covering on the face; Ex. barefaced lie</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) with no effort to conceal
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bald">bald</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  a barefaced lie               </li>

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
