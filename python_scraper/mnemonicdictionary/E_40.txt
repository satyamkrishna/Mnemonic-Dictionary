      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : risk, particularly of being exposed to disease or to the elements; unmasking; act of laying something open; Ex. exposure of governmental corruption</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) vulnerability to the elements; to the action of heat or cold or wind or rain
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
