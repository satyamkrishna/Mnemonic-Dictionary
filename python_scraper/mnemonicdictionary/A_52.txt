      <h2>askance</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : with a sideways or indirect look (with disapproval or distruct); Ex. look askance at</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (used especially of glances) directed to one side with or as if with doubt or suspicion or envy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/askant">askant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/asquint">asquint</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sidelong">sidelong</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/squint">squint</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/squint-eyed">squint-eyed</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/squinty">squinty</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  her eyes with their misted askance look               </li>
                              <li>
                  sidelong glances               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adv) with suspicion or disapproval
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he looked askance at the offer               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adv) with a side or oblique glance
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  did not quite turn all the way back but looked askance at me with her dark eyes               </li>

      <h2>askew</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : crookedly; slanted; at an angle</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) turned or twisted toward one side
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/awry">awry</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cockeyed">cockeyed</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lopsided">lopsided</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/skew-whiff">skew-whiff</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/wonky">wonky</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a...youth with a gorgeous red necktie all awry               </li>

                  his wig was, as the British say, skew-whiff               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adv) turned or twisted to one side
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
