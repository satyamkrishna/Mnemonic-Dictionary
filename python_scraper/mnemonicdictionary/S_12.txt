      <h2>scurry</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : move hastily; hurry; move briskly</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) rushing about hastily in an undignified way
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scamper">scamper</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scramble">scramble</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) to move about or proceed hurriedly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scamper">scamper</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scuttle">scuttle</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/skitter">skitter</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : burn the surface of; char or burn; brand; parch; cause (a plant) to wither</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make very hot and dry
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scorch">scorch</a>

         </div>

                  The cook blackened the chicken breast               </li>

                  the flames scorched the ceiling               </li>
                           </ul>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to wither or parch from exposure to heat
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/parch">parch</a>
