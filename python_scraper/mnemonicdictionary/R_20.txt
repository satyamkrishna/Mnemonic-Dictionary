      <h2>relegate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : put into a lower or worse place; banish to an inferior position; delegate; assign; Ex. relegate the old furniture to the children's room; Ex. relegated to the second division</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) refer to another person for decision or judgment
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pass on">pass on</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/submit">submit</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) assign to a lower position; reduce in rank
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/break">break</a>
