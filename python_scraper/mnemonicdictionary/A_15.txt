      <h2>adjunct</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : something attached to but holding an inferior position</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something added to another thing but not an essential part of it
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who is an assistant or subordinate to another
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a construction that can be used to extend the meaning of a word or phrase but is not one of the main constituents of a sentence
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) furnishing added support
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/accessory">accessory</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adjuvant">adjuvant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ancillary">ancillary</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/appurtenant">appurtenant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/auxiliary">auxiliary</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an ancillary pump               </li>
                              <li>
                  an adjuvant discipline to forms of mysticism               </li>
                              <li>
                  The mind and emotions are auxiliary to each other               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to a person who is subordinate to another
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/assistant">assistant</a>

            
             
         </div>
