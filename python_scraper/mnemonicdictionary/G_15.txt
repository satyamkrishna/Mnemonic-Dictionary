      <h2>goad</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : urge on; drive with a goad; cause (someone) to do something by continued annoyance; Ex. They goaded him into doing it by saying he was a coward; N: sharp-pointed stick for driving cattle; stimulus; CF</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a pointed instrument that is used to prod into a state of motion
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prod">prod</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a verbalization that encourages you to attempt something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/goading">goading</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prod">prod</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prodding">prodding</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spur">spur</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spurring">spurring</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/urging">urging</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the ceaseless prodding got on his nerves               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) give heart or courage to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spur">spur</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) urge with or as if with a goad
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) stab or urge on as if with a pointed stick
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prick">prick</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) goad or provoke,as by constant criticism
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/needle">needle</a>
