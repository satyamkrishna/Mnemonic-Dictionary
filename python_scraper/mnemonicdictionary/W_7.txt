      <h2>whiff</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : puff or gust (of air, scent, etc.); short-lasting smell; hint; Ex. whiff of perfume/scandal</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a short light gust of air
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/puff">puff</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/puff of air">puff of air</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a lefteye flounder found in coastal waters from New England to Brazil
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a strikeout resulting from the batter swinging at and missing the ball for the third strike
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) perceive by inhaling through the nose
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sniff">sniff</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) drive or carry as if by a puff of air
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The gust of air whiffed away the clouds               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) strike out by swinging and missing the pitch charged as the third
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) smoke and exhale strongly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/puff">puff</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  whiff a pipe               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) utter with a puff of air
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

      <h2>whimsical</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : capricious; fanciful; amusingly strange</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) determined by chance or impulse or whim rather than by necessity or reason
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/capricious">capricious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/impulsive">impulsive</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a capricious refusal               </li>
                              <li>
                  authoritarian rulers are frequently capricious               </li>
                              <li>
                  the victim of whimsical persecutions               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
