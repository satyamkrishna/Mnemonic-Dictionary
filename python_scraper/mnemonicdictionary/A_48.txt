      <h2>arid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of land) dry; barren; unproductive</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking sufficient water or rainfall
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/waterless">waterless</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an arid climate               </li>
                              <li>
                  a waterless well               </li>
                              <li>
                  miles of waterless country to cross               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking vitality or spirit; lifeless
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/desiccate">desiccate</a>
