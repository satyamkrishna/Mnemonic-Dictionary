      <h2>supine</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lying on back; passive; inactive; Ex. The defeated pugilist lay supine; Ex. supine acceptance of the decision</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lying face upward
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resupine">resupine</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) offering no resistance

      <h2>supplant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : take the place of unfairly; usurp; replace</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) take the place or move into the position of
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/replace">replace</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/supercede">supercede</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/supersede">supersede</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/supervene upon">supervene upon</a>

            
             
         </div>
