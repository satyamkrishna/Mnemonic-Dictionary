      <h2>necromancy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : black magic; sorcery; dealings with the dead; art that professes to communicate with the spirits of the dead so as to predict the future; CF. necromancer; CF. necro+divination; CF. necro-: death; Ex.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the belief in magical spells that harness occult forces or evil spirits to produce unnatural effects in the world
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/black art">black art</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/black magic">black magic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sorcery">sorcery</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
