      <h2>libel</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : defamatory written statement; act of writing something that smears a person's character; V. ADJ. libelous</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a false and malicious publication printed for the purpose of defaming a living person
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the written statement of a plaintiff explaining the cause of action (the defamation) and any relief he seeks
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) print slanderous statements against
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The newspaper was accused of libeling him               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
