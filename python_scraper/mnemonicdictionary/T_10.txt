      <h2>terminal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : part that forms the end; railroad or bus station; ADJ. forming an end; ending in death; fatal; Ex. terminal cancer</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) station where transport vehicles load or unload passengers or goods
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/depot">depot</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/terminus">terminus</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a contact on an electrical device (such as a battery) at which electric current enters or leaves
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pole">pole</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) either extremity of something that has length
