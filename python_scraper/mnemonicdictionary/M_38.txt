      <h2>murmur</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : low, indistinct, continuous sound; V. CF. mumble</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a low continuous indistinct sound; often accompanied by movement of the lips without the production of articulate speech
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/murmuration">murmuration</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/murmuring">murmuring</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mussitation">mussitation</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mutter">mutter</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/muttering">muttering</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a schwa that is incidental to the pronunciation of a consonant
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
