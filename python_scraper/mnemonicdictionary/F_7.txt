      <h2>feign</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pretend</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make believe with the intent to deceive
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/affect">affect</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dissemble">dissemble</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pretend">pretend</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sham">sham</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  He feigned that he was ill               </li>

                  He shammed a headache               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
