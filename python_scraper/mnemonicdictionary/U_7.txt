      <h2>unique</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : without an equal; single in kind</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) radically distinctive and without equal
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/alone">alone</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unequaled">unequaled</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unequalled">unequalled</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unparalleled">unparalleled</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he is alone in the field of microbiology               </li>
                              <li>
                  this theory is altogether alone in its penetration of the problem               </li>
                              <li>
                  Bach was unique in his handling of counterpoint               </li>
                              <li>
                  craftsmen whose skill is unequaled               </li>
                              <li>
                  unparalleled athletic ability               </li>
                              <li>
                  a breakdown of law unparalleled in our history               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (followed by `to') applying exclusively to a given category or condition or locality
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a species unique to Australia               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) the single one of its kind
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/singular">singular</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a singular example               </li>
                              <li>
                  the unique existing example of Donne's handwriting               </li>
                              <li>
                  a unique copy of an ancient manuscript               </li>
                              <li>
                  certain types of problems have unique solutions               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) highly unusual or rare but not the single instance
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spoke with a unique accent               </li>
                              <li>
                  had unique ability in raising funds               </li>
                              <li>
                  a frankness unique in literature               </li>
                              <li>
                  a unique dining experience               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>unison</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unity of pitch (in musical performance); complete accord; Ex. The choir sang in unison.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) corresponding exactly
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  marching in unison               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) occurring together or simultaneously
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the two spoke in unison               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (music) two or more sounds or tones at the same pitch or in octaves
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  singing in unison               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>universal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : characterizing or affecting all; general; present everywhere; of the universe; cosmic; Ex. universal agreement; Ex. a subject of universal interest</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (linguistics) a grammatical rule (or other linguistic feature) that is found in all languages
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/linguistic universal">linguistic universal</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (logic) a proposition that asserts something of all members of a class
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/universal proposition">universal proposition</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a behavioral convention or pattern characteristic of all members of a particular culture or of all human beings
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  some form of religion seems to be a human universal               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) coupling that connects two rotating shafts allowing freedom of movement in all directions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/universal joint">universal joint</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  in motor vehicles a universal joint allows the driveshaft to move up and down as the vehicle passes over bumps               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of worldwide scope or applicability
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cosmopolitan">cosmopolitan</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ecumenical">ecumenical</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/general">general</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/oecumenical">oecumenical</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/world-wide">world-wide</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/worldwide">worldwide</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an issue of cosmopolitan import               </li>
                              <li>
                  the shrewdest political and ecumenical comment of our time               </li>
                              <li>
                  universal experience               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) applicable to or common to all members of a group or set
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the play opened to universal acclaim               </li>
                              <li>
                  rap enjoys universal appeal among teenage boys               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) adapted to various purposes, sizes, forms, operations
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  universal wrench               </li>
                              <li>
                  universal chuck               </li>
                              <li>
                  universal screwdriver               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>unkempt</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : disheveled; uncared for in appearance; not combed; CF. comb</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not neatly combed
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  wild unkempt hair               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>unmitigated</h2>
