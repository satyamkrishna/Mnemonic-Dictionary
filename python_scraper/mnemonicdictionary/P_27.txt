      <h2>Philistine</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : narrow-minded person, uncultured and exclusively interested in material gain; uncultured person who is indifferent to artistic and cultural values; member of an ancient people in Palestine; OP. aesthe</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who is uninterested in intellectual pursuits
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/anti-intellectual">anti-intellectual</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lowbrow">lowbrow</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a member of an Aegean people who settled ancient Philistia around the 12th century BC
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to ancient Philistia or its culture or its people
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) smug and ignorant and indifferent or hostile to artistic and cultural values
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/anti-intellectual">anti-intellectual</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
