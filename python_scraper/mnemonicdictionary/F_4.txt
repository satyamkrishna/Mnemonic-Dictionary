      <h2>fanciful</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : whimsical; visionary; imaginary; produced by imagination; Ex. fanciful scheme</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) indulging in or influenced by fancy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/notional">notional</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a fanciful mind               </li>
                              <li>
