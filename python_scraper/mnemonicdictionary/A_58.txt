         (adj) invulnerable to fear or intimidation

      <h2>audit</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : examination of accounts of a business; official examination; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an inspection of the accounting procedures and records by a trained accountant or CPA
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/audited account">audited account</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a methodical examination or review of a condition or situation
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he made an audit of all the plants on his property               </li>
                              <li>
                  an energy efficiency audit               </li>
                              <li>
                  an email log audit               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) examine carefully for accuracy with the intent of verification
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inspect">inspect</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scrutinise">scrutinise</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scrutinize">scrutinize</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  audit accounts and tax returns               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) attend academic courses without getting credit

      <h2>aureole</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sun's corona; halo; bright circle of light</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the outermost region of the sun's atmosphere; visible as a white halo during a solar eclipse
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
