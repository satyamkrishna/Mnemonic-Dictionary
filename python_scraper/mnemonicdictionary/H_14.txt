      <h2>holocaust</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : destruction by fire; CF. burnt whole; CF. Holocaust</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an act of mass destruction and loss of life (especially in war or by fire)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  a nuclear holocaust               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the mass murder of Jews under the German Nazi regime from 1941 until 1945
