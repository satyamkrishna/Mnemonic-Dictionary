      <h2>divest</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : strip (as of clothes); deprive (as of rights); dispossess; N. divestiture(divestment)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) take away possessions from someone
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deprive">deprive</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/strip">strip</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The Nazis stripped the Jews of all their assets               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) deprive of status or authority
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disinvest">disinvest</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he was divested of his rights and his title               </li>
                              <li>
                  They disinvested themselves of their rights               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) reduce or dispose of; cease to hold (an investment)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disinvest">disinvest</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The company decided to divest               </li>
                              <li>
                  the board of trustees divested $20 million in real estate property               </li>
                              <li>
                  There was pressure on the university to disinvest in South Africa               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) remove (someone's or one's own) clothes
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disinvest">disinvest</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/strip">strip</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/undress">undress</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  The nurse quickly undressed the accident victim               </li>

      <h2>doctrinaire</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unable to compromise about points of doctrine; dogmatic; unyielding; marked by inflexible attachment to a doctrine without regard to its practical difficulties</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a stubborn person of arbitrary or arrogant opinions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
