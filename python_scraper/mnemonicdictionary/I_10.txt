      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not being a person; not showing personal feelings; Ex. impersonal force/manner/organization</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not relating to or responsive to individual persons
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>impertinent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : insolent; rude; not pertinent; N. impertinence</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by a lightly pert and exuberant quality
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/irreverent">irreverent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pert">pert</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/saucy">saucy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a certain irreverent gaiety and ease of manner               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not pertinent to the matter under consideration
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/extraneous">extraneous</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/immaterial">immaterial</a>

         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  an issue extraneous to the debate               </li>
                              <li>
                  the price was immaterial               </li>

      <h2>imperturbable</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unshakably calm; placid</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not easily perturbed or excited or upset; marked by extreme calm and composure
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unflappable">unflappable</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  hitherto imperturbable, he now showed signs of alarm               </li>
                              <li>
                  an imperturbable self-possession               </li>
                              <li>
