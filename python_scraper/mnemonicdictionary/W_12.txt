      <h2>woeful</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sad; (of something bad) deplorable; deplorably bad; Ex. woeful housing conditions</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) affected by or full of grief or woe
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/woebegone">woebegone</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of very poor quality or condition
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deplorable">deplorable</a>
