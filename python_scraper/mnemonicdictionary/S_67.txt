      <h2>supererogatory</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : superfluous; more than needed or demanded</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) more than is needed, desired, or required
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/excess">excess</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/extra">extra</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/redundant">redundant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/surplus">surplus</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>superficial</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : of the surface; not deep; shallow; not thorough; trivial; Ex. superficial analysis/knowledge</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) concerned with or comprehending only what is apparent or obvious; not deep or penetrating emotionally or intellectually
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  superficial similarities               </li>
                              <li>
                  a superficial mind               </li>
                              <li>
                  his thinking was superficial and fuzzy               </li>
                              <li>
                  superficial knowledge               </li>
                              <li>
                  the superficial report didn't give the true picture               </li>
                              <li>
                  only superficial differences               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of, affecting, or being on or near the surface
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  superficial measurements               </li>
                              <li>
                  the superficial area of the wall               </li>
                              <li>
                  a superficial wound               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of little substance or significance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trivial">trivial</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a few superficial editorial changes               </li>
                              <li>
                  only trivial objections               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
