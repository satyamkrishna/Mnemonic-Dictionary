      <h2>untoward</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unexpected and adverse; unfortunate or unlucky; Ex. untoward encounter</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not in keeping with accepted standards of what is right or proper in polite society
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/indecent">indecent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/indecorous">indecorous</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unbecoming">unbecoming</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/uncomely">uncomely</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unseemly">unseemly</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  was buried with indecent haste               </li>
                              <li>
                  indecorous behavior               </li>
                              <li>
                  language unbecoming to a lady               </li>
                              <li>
                  unseemly to use profanity               </li>
                              <li>
                  moved to curb their untoward ribaldry               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) contrary to your interests or welfare
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adverse">adverse</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inauspicious">inauspicious</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  adverse circumstances               </li>
                              <li>
                  made a place for themselves under the most untoward conditions               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
