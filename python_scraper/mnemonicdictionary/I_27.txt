      <h2>indifferent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unmoved or unconcerned by; having no interest in; mediocre; neither good nor bad</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by a lack of interest
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/apathetic">apathetic</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an apathetic audience               </li>
                              <li>
                  the universe is neither hostile nor friendly; it is simply indifferent               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) showing no care or concern in attitude or action
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  indifferent to the sufferings of others               </li>
                              <li>
                  indifferent to her plea               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (usually followed by `to') unwilling or refusing to pay heed
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deaf">deaf</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  deaf to her warnings               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (often followed by `to') lacking importance; not mattering one way or the other
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/immaterial">immaterial</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  whether you choose to do it or not is a matter that is quite immaterial (or indifferent)               </li>
                              <li>
                  what others think is altogether indifferent to him               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) fairly poor to not very good
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  has an indifferent singing voice               </li>
                              <li>
                  has indifferent qualifications for the job               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having only a limited ability to react chemically; chemically inactive
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inert">inert</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/neutral">neutral</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  inert matter               </li>
                              <li>
                  an indifferent chemical in a reaction               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by no especial liking or dislike or preference for one thing over another
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  indifferent about which book you would give them               </li>
                              <li>
                  was indifferent to their acceptance or rejection of her invitation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by a lack of partiality
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unbiased">unbiased</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unbiassed">unbiassed</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a properly indifferent jury               </li>
                              <li>
                  an unbiasgoted account of her family problems               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being neither good nor bad
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/so-so">so-so</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an indifferent performance               </li>
                              <li>
                  a gifted painter but an indifferent actor               </li>
                              <li>
                  her work at the office is passable               </li>
                              <li>
                  a so-so golfer               </li>
                              <li>
                  feeling only so-so               </li>
                              <li>
                  prepared a tolerable dinner               </li>
                              <li>
                  a tolerable working knowledge of French               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) neither too great nor too little
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a couple of indifferent hills to climb               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
