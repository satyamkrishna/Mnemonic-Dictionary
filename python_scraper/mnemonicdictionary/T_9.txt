      <h2>tensile</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : capable of being stretched; of tension; Ex. tensile rubber</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to tension
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  tensile stress               </li>
                              <li>
                  tensile pull               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) capable of being shaped or bent or drawn out

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ductile">ductile</a>

      <h2>tension</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : stretching; condition of being stretched; mental strain; strained relationship between groups or people</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (psychology) a state of mental or emotional strain or suspense
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stress">stress</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tenseness">tenseness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he suffered from fatigue and emotional tension               </li>
                              <li>
                  stress is a vasoconstrictor               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the physical condition of being stretched or strained
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tautness">tautness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tenseness">tenseness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tensity">tensity</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  it places great tension on the leg muscles               </li>
                              <li>
                  he could feel the tenseness of her body               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a balance between and interplay of opposing elements or tendencies (especially in art or literature)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  there is a tension created between narrative time and movie time               </li>
                              <li>
                  there is a tension between these approaches to understanding history               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (physics) a stress that produces an elongation of an elastic physical body
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the direction of maximum tension moves asymptotically toward the direction of the shear               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) feelings of hostility that are not manifest
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/latent hostility">latent hostility</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he could sense her latent hostility to him               </li>
                              <li>
                  the diplomats' first concern was to reduce international tensions               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the action of stretching something tight
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  tension holds the belt in the pulleys               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>tentative</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not fully worked out or developed; provisional; experimental; uncertain; hesitant; not definite or positive; Ex. tentative agreement/reply</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) under terms not final or fully worked out or agreed upon
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/probationary">probationary</a>
