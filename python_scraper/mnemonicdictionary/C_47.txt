      <h2>concomitant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : that which accompanies; Ex. Deafnes is a frequent concommitant of old age; ADJ: existing or happening together with something else</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
