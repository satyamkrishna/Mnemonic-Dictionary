      <h2>lackadaisical</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lacking interest or effort; lacking purpose or zest; lazy; halfhearted; languid</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking spirit or liveliness
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dreamy">dreamy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/languid">languid</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/languorous">languorous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a lackadaisical attempt               </li>
                              <li>
                  a languid mood               </li>
                              <li>
                  a languid wave of the hand               </li>
                              <li>
                  a hot languorous afternoon               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) idle or indolent especially in a dreamy way

      <h2>lackluster</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : lacking luster(shine; gloss); dull</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking brilliance or vitality
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lacklustre">lacklustre</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lusterless">lusterless</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lustreless">lustreless</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a dull lackluster life               </li>
                              <li>
                  a lusterless performance               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
