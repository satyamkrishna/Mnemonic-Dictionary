      <h2>abstract</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : theoretical; not concrete; nonrepresentational</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a concept or idea not associated with any specific instance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abstraction">abstraction</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he loved her only in the abstract--not in person               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sketchy summary of the main points of an argument or theory
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/outline">outline</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/precis">precis</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/synopsis">synopsis</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) consider a concept without thinking of a specific example; consider abstractly or theoretically
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>abysmal</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
