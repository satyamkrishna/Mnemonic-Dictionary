      <h2>aberrant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : abnormal or deviant</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) one whose behavior departs substantially from the norm of a group
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) markedly different from an accepted norm
