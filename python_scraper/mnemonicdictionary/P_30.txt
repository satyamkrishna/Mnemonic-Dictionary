      <h2>pine</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : long for; yearn; languish from longing or grief; decline</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a coniferous tree
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pine tree">pine tree</a>

         (noun) straight-grained durable and often resinous white to yellowish timber of any of numerous trees of the genus Pinus
