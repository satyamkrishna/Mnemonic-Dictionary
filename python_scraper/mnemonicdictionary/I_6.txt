      <h2>impalpable</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : imperceptible(not easily understood); intangible; OP. palpable: tangible; easily perceptible</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) incapable of being perceived by the senses especially the sense of touch
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/intangible">intangible</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the intangible constituent of energy               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) imperceptible to the senses or the mind
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an impalpable cloud               </li>
                              <li>
                  impalpable shadows               </li>
