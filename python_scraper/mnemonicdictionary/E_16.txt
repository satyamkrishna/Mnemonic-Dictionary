      <h2>endemic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : prevailing among a specific group of people or in a specific area or country; peculiar to a particular region or people; CF. pandemic</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a disease that is constantly present to a greater or lesser degree in people of a certain class or in people living in a particular location
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/endemic disease">endemic disease</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a plant that is native to a certain limited area
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  it is an endemic found only this island               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to a disease (or anything resembling a disease) constantly present to greater or lesser extent in a particular locality
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/endemical">endemical</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  diseases endemic to the tropics               </li>

                  endemic malaria               </li>
                              <li>
                  food shortages and starvation are endemic in certain parts of the world               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) native to or confined to a certain region
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the islands have a number of interesting endemic species               </li>
                           </ul>
