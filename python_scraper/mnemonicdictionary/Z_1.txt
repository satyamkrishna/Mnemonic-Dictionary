      <h2>zeal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : eager enthusiasm (to a cause or ideal); ADJ. zealous</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a feeling of strong eagerness (usually in favor of a person or cause)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ardor">ardor</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ardour">ardour</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/elan">elan</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  they were imbued with a revolutionary ardor               </li>
                              <li>
                  he felt a kind of religious zeal               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) excessive fervor to do something or accomplish some end
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he had an absolute zeal for litigation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) prompt willingness
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/eagerness">eagerness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forwardness">forwardness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/readiness">readiness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  readiness to continue discussions               </li>
                              <li>
                  they showed no eagerness to spread the gospel               </li>
                              <li>
                  they disliked his zeal in demonstrating his superiority               </li>
                              <li>
