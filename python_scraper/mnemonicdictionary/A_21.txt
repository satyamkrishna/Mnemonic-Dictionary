      <h2>agnostic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : one who is skeptical of the existence or knowability of a god or any ultimate reality</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who is doubtful or noncommittal about something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/doubter">doubter</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who claims that they cannot have true knowledge about the existence of God (but does not deny that God might exist)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or pertaining to an agnostic or agnosticism
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) uncertain of all claims to knowledge
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/agnostical">agnostical</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
