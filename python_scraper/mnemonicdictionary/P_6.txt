      <h2>papyrus</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : ancient paper made from stem of papyrus plant</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) paper made from the papyrus plant by cutting it in strips and pressing it flat; used by ancient Egyptians and Greeks and Romans
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) tall sedge of the Nile valley yielding fiber that served many purposes in historic times
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cyperus papyrus">cyperus papyrus</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/egyptian paper reed">egyptian paper reed</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/egyptian paper rush">egyptian paper rush</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/paper plant">paper plant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/paper rush">paper rush</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a document written on papyrus

      <h2>paradigm</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : model; example that serves as a model; pattern; list of all the inflectional forms of a word</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) systematic arrangement of all the inflected forms of a word
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a standard or typical example
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/epitome">epitome</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/image">image</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prototype">prototype</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he is the prototype of good breeding               </li>
                              <li>
                  he provided America with an image of the good father               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the class of all items that can be substituted into the same position (or slot) in a grammatical sentence (are in paradigmatic relation with one another)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/substitution class">substitution class</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the generally accepted perspective of a particular discipline at a given time
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he framed the problem within the psychoanalytic paradigm               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
