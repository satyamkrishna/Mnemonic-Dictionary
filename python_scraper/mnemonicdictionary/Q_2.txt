      <h2>qualified</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : limited; restricted; V. qualify: limit the meaning of; modify</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) meeting the proper standards and requirements and training for an office or position or task
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  many qualified applicants for the job               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) limited or restricted; not absolute
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  gave only qualified approval               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) holding appropriate documentation and officially on record as qualified to perform a specified function or practice a specified skill

      <h2>quandary</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : dilemma; state of uncertainty; Ex. She is in a quandary about whether to go.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a situation from which extrication is difficult especially an unpleasant or trying one
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/plight">plight</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/predicament">predicament</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  finds himself in a most awkward predicament               </li>
                              <li>
                  the woeful plight of homeless people               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) state of uncertainty or perplexity especially as requiring a choice between equally unfavorable options
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dilemma">dilemma</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
