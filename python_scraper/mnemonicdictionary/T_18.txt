      <h2>token</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : outward sign; Ex. a token of our gratitude</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an individual instance of a type of symbol
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/item">item</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the word`error' contains three tokens of `r'               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something serving as a sign of something else
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a metal or plastic disk that can be redeemed or used in designated slot machines
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something of sentimental value
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/keepsake">keepsake</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/relic">relic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/souvenir">souvenir</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) insignificantly small; a matter of form only (`tokenish' is informal)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nominal">nominal</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tokenish">tokenish</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the fee was nominal               </li>
                              <li>
                  a token gesture of resistance               </li>
                              <li>
                  a toknenish gesture               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>toll</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sound (a large bell) slowly at regular intervals; N: sound of a bell; fixed tax or charge</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a fee levied for the use of roads or bridges (used for maintenance)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) value measured by what must be given or done or undergone to obtain something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cost">cost</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/price">price</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the cost in human life was enormous               </li>
                              <li>
                  the price of success is hard work               </li>
                              <li>
                  what price glory?               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the sound of a bell being struck
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bell">bell</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  saved by the bell               </li>
                              <li>
                  she heard the distant toll of church bells               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) ring slowly
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  For whom the bell tolls               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) charge a fee for using
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Toll the bridges into New York City               </li>
