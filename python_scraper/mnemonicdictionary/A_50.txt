      <h2>artful</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : exhibiting art or skill; deceitful; cunning; CF. artifice</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not straightforward or candid; giving a false appearance of frankness
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disingenuous">disingenuous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an ambitious, disingenuous, philistine, and hypocritical operator, who...exemplified...the most disagreeable traits of his time               </li>
                              <li>
                  a disingenuous excuse               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by skill in achieving a desired end especially with cunning or craft
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the artful dodger               </li>
                              <li>
                  an artful choice of metaphors               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

      <h2>articulate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : effective; distinct; expressing ideas clearly; having clear sounds; having joints; Ex. articulate speech; V: express thoughts and feeling clearly; pronounce clearly; unite by joints</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) provide with a joint
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/joint">joint</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the carpenter jointed two pieces of wood               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) put into words or an expression
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/formulate">formulate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/give voice">give voice</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/phrase">phrase</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/word">word</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He formulated his concerns to the board of trustees               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) speak, pronounce, or utter in a certain way
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/enounce">enounce</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/enunciate">enunciate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pronounce">pronounce</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/say">say</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sound out">sound out</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She pronounces French words in a funny way               </li>
                              <li>
                  I cannot say `zip wire'               </li>
                              <li>
                  Can the child sound out this complicated word?               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) unite by forming a joint or joints
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the ankle bone articulates with the leg bones to form the ankle bones               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) express or state clearly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/enunciate">enunciate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vocalise">vocalise</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vocalize">vocalize</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) expressing yourself easily or characterized by clear expressive language
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  articulate speech               </li>
                              <li>
                  an articulate orator               </li>
                              <li>
                  articulate beings               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) consisting of segments held together by joints

            <u>Synonyms</u> :
