      <h2>consolidation</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) combining into a solid mass
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of combining into an integral whole
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/integration">integration</a>

      <h2>consonant</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : harmonious; in agreement; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
