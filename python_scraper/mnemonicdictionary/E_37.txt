      <h2>exigency</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : urgent situation; ADJ. exigent</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a pressing or urgent situation
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the health-care exigency               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sudden unforeseen crisis (usually involving danger) that requires immediate action
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/emergency">emergency</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pinch">pinch</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he never knew what to do in an emergency               </li>
                           </ul>

      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of costs or demands) excessive; exceeding reasonable bounds</p>
