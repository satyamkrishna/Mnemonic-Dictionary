      <h2>stultify</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make stupid in mind; cause to appear or become stupid or inconsistent; suppress; frustrate or hinder; Ex. stultifying effect of uninteresting work; Ex. stultify free expression</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) prove to be of unsound mind or demonstrate someone's incompetence
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  nobody is legally allowed to stultify himself               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to appear foolish
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He stultified himself by contradicting himself and being inconsistent               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) deprive of strength or efficiency; make useless or worthless
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cripple">cripple</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  This measure crippled our efforts               </li>
                              <li>
                  Their behavior stultified the boss's hard work               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>stumble</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : trip and almost fall; proceed unsteadily; act falteringly; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unsteady uneven gait
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lurch">lurch</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stagger">stagger</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unintentional but embarrassing blunder
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/misstep">misstep</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trip">trip</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trip-up">trip-up</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  he recited the whole poem without a single trip               </li>
                              <li>
                  he arranged his robes to avoid a trip-up later               </li>
                              <li>
                  confusion caused his unfortunate misstep               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) walk unsteadily
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bumble">bumble</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/falter">falter</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The drunk man stumbled about               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) miss a step and fall or nearly fall
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trip">trip</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) encounter by chance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hit">hit</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                           </ul>
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />
