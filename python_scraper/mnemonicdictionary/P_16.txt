      <h2>peer</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : one who has equal standing with another; nobleman; V: look intently; N. peerage; CF. peeress</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who is of equal standing with another in a group
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/compeer">compeer</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/equal">equal</a>

         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a nobleman (duke or marquis or earl or viscount or baron) who is a member of the British peerage
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) look searchingly
