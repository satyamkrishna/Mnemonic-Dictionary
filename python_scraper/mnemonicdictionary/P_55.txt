         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) open to doubt or debate
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :

      <h2>procrastinate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : postpone; delay or put off</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) postpone doing what one should be doing
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dilly-dally">dilly-dally</a>

         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
