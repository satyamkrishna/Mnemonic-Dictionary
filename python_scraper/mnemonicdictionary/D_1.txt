      <h2>dabble</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : work at in a nonserious fashion; splash around; move noisily in a liquid</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) dip a foot or hand briefly into a liquid
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) play in or as if in water, as of small children
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/paddle">paddle</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/splash around">splash around</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) work with in an amateurish manner
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/play around">play around</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/smatter">smatter</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She dabbles in astronomy               </li>
                              <li>
                  He plays around with investments but he never makes any money               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) bob forward and under so as to feed off the bottom of a body of water
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
