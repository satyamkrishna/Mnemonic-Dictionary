      <h2>reconcile</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make friendly again (after quarrel); make consistent (two ideas in opposition); correct inconsistencies; Ex. reconcile one's political principles with one's religious beliefs</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make (one thing) compatible with (another)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/accommodate">accommodate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conciliate">conciliate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The scientists had to accommodate the new results with the existing theories               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) bring into consonance or accord
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/harmonise">harmonise</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/harmonize">harmonize</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  harmonize one's goals with one's abilities               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) come to terms
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conciliate">conciliate</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/make up">make up</a>

      <h2>recondite</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : abstruse; not easily understood; profound; secret</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) difficult to penetrate; incomprehensible to one of ordinary understanding or knowledge
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abstruse">abstruse</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deep">deep</a>

            
             
         </div>
