      <h2>intrude</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : put or force in without being asked; trespass; enter as an uninvited person; Ex. intrude one's own opinion into the report; CF. thrust in</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) enter uninvited
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/irrupt">irrupt</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  They intruded on our dinner party               </li>
                              <li>
                  She irrupted into our sitting room               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) enter unlawfully on someone's property
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trespass">trespass</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Don't trespass on my land!               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) search or inquire in a meddlesome way
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/horn in">horn in</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nose">nose</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/poke">poke</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pry">pry</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  This guy is always nosing around the office               </li>

         </div>

      <h2>intuition</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : immediate insight; power of knowing without reasoning; ADJ. intuitive; V. intuit: know by intuition</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) instinctive knowing (without the use of rational processes)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>inundate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : flood; overflow; submerge; cover completely; Ex. inundated with work</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) fill quickly beyond capacity; as with a liquid
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deluge">deluge</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flood">flood</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/swamp">swamp</a>

            
             
         </div>

                  The images flooded his mind               </li>
                           </ul>

         <u>Definition</u><br />
