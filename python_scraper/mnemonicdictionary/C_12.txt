      <h2>catastrophe</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : calamity ; disaster</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an event resulting in great loss and misfortune
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/calamity">calamity</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cataclysm">cataclysm</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disaster">disaster</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tragedy">tragedy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the whole city was affected by the irremediable calamity               </li>
                              <li>
                  the earthquake was a disaster               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a state of extreme (usually irremediable) ruin and misfortune
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disaster">disaster</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  lack of funds has resulted in a catastrophe for our school system               </li>
                              <li>
                  his policies were a disaster               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sudden violent change in the earth's surface
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cataclysm">cataclysm</a>
