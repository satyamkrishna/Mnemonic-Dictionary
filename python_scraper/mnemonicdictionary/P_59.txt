      <h2>prominent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : protruding(sticking out); conspicuous; notable; eminent</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having a quality that thrusts itself into attention
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/outstanding">outstanding</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/salient">salient</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spectacular">spectacular</a>

                        ,

         </div>
