      <h2>posterity</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : descendants; future generations; Ex. go down to posterity; CF. posterior, anterior</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) all of the offspring of a given progenitor
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/descendants">descendants</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>postmortem</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : autopsy; ADJ: occurring after death; of a postmortem</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) discussion of an event after it has occurred
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>postulate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : self-evident truth; something assumed without proof; V: assume the truth of (as a basis of an argument)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (logic) a proposition that is accepted as true in order to provide a basis for logical reasoning
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/posit">posit</a>

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/contend">contend</a>

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/posit">posit</a>
