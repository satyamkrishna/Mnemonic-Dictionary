      <h2>refraction</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : bending of a ray of light</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the change in direction of a propagating wave (light or sound) when passing from one medium to another
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the amount by which a propagating wave is bent

      <h2>refractory</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unmanageable; disobedient and stubborn; Ex. refractory horse</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) lining consisting of material with a high melting point; used to line the inside walls of a furnace

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/furnace lining">furnace lining</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not responding to treatment
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stubborn">stubborn</a>

            
             
         </div>
