      <h2>lumber</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : move heavily or clumsily; Ex. The bear lumbered through the woods; N: timber</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the wood of trees cut and prepared for use as building material
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/timber">timber</a>

            
             
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an implement used in baseball by the batter

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/baseball bat">baseball bat</a>

      <h2>lumen</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unit of light energy (one candle's worth)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a unit of luminous flux equal to the amount of light given out through a solid angle of 1 steradian by a point source of 1 candela intensity radiating uniformly in all directions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lm">lm</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a cavity or passage in a tubular organ
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the lumen of the intestine               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
