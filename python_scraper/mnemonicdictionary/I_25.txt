      <h2>incursion</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) the act of entering some territory or domain (often in large numbers)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an attack that penetrates into enemy territory
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/penetration">penetration</a>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>indecision</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : irresolution; inability to make up one's mind</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) doubt concerning two or more possible alternatives or courses of action
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/indecisiveness">indecisiveness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/irresolution">irresolution</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his indecision was only momentary but the opportunity was lost               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the trait of irresolution; a lack of firmness of character or purpose
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/indecisiveness">indecisiveness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
