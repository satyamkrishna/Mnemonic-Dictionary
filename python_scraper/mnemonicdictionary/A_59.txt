      <h2>austere</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : forbiddingly stern; ascetic; without comfort or enjoyment; severely simple and unornamented; Ex. a monk's austere life; Ex. austere grandeur of the cathedral; N. austerity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) severely simple
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/severe">severe</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stark">stark</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stern">stern</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a stark interior               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of a stern or strict bearing or demeanor; forbidding in aspect
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stern">stern</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an austere expression               </li>
                              <li>
                  a stern face               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) practicing great self-denial
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ascetic">ascetic</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ascetical">ascetical</a>

                        ,

         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  a desert nomad's austere life               </li>
