      <h2>excise</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : cut away; cut out; N: government tax on good produced and used inside a country; N. excision</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a tax that is measured by the amount of business done (not on property or income from real estate)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/excise tax">excise tax</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) remove by erasing or crossing out or as if by drawing a line
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/expunge">expunge</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scratch">scratch</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/strike">strike</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Please strike this remark from the record               </li>
                              <li>
                  scratch that remark               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) levy an excise tax on
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) remove by cutting
