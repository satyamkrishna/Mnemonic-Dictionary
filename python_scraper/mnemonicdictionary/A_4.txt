      <h2>aboriginal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : being the first of its kind in a region; primitive; native; indigenous; N. aborigine</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a dark-skinned member of a race of people living in Australia when Europeans arrived
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abo">abo</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/native australian">native australian</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an indigenous person who was born in a particular place
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aborigine">aborigine</a>
