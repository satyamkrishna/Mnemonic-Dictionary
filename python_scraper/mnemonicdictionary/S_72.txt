      <h2>swamp</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : wetland; marsh; V: flood; overwhelm; drench in with liquid</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) low land that is seasonally flooded; has more woody plants than a marsh and better drainage than a bog
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/swampland">swampland</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a situation fraught with difficulties and imponderables
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he was trapped in a medical swamp               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) drench or submerge or be drenched or submerged
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/drench">drench</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deluge">deluge</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flood">flood</a>
