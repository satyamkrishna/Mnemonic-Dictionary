      <h2>edifice</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : building (of imposing size)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a structure that has a roof and walls and stands more or less permanently in one place
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/building">building</a>
