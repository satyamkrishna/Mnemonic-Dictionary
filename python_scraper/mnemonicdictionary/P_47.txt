      <h2>predicament</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : difficult situation; tricky or dangerous situation; dilemma</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a situation from which extrication is difficult especially an unpleasant or trying one
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/plight">plight</a>

         </div>

      <h2>predilection</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : preference; partiality</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a predisposition in favor of something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/orientation">orientation</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/preference">preference</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a predilection for expensive cars               </li>
                              <li>
                  his sexual preferences               </li>
                              <li>
                  showed a Marxist orientation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a strong liking
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/penchant">penchant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/preference">preference</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/taste">taste</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  my own preference is for good literature               </li>
                              <li>
                  the Irish have a penchant for blarney               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
