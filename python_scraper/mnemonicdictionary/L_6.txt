      <h2>latitude</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : freedom from narrow limitations</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the angular distance between an imaginary line around a heavenly body parallel to its equator and the equator itself
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) freedom from normal restraints in conduct
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the new freedom in movies and novels               </li>
                              <li>
                  allowed his children considerable latitude in how they spent their money               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an imaginary line around the Earth parallel to the equator
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/line of latitude">line of latitude</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/parallel">parallel</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/parallel of latitude">parallel of latitude</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) scope for freedom of e.g. action or thought; freedom from restriction
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
