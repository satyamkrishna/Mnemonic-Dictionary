         (noun) rewording for the purpose of clarification

      <h2>parasite</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : animal or plant living on another; toady; sycophant; CF. para-: beside</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an animal or plant that lives in or on a host (another animal or plant); it obtains nourishment from the host without benefiting or killing the host
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a follower who hangs around a host (without benefit to the host) in hope of gain or advantage
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/leech">leech</a>

      <h2>parched</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : extremely dry; very thirsty; V. parch: make or become extremely dry (by exposure to heat)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) dried out by heat or excessive exposure to sunlight
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adust">adust</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/baked">baked</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scorched">scorched</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sunbaked">sunbaked</a>

            
             
         </div>
