      <h2>integral</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : complete; necessary for completeness; Ex. integral part</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the result of a mathematical integration; F(x) is the integral of f(x) if dF/dx = f(x)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) existing as an essential constituent or characteristic
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/built-in">built-in</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/constitutional">constitutional</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inbuilt">inbuilt</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inherent">inherent</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the Ptolemaic system with its built-in concept of periodicity               </li>
                              <li>
                  a constitutional inability to tell the truth               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) constituting the undiminished entirety; lacking nothing essential especially not damaged
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/entire">entire</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/intact">intact</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a local motion keepeth bodies integral               </li>
                              <li>
                  was able to keep the collection entire during his lifetime               </li>
                              <li>
                  fought to keep the union intact               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or denoted by an integer
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
