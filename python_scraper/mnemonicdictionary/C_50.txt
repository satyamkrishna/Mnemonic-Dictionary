      <h2>confiscate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : seize; take possession of (private property) by official order (usu. as a punishment); commandeer</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) take temporary possession of as a security, by legal authority
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/attach">attach</a>

         </div>
