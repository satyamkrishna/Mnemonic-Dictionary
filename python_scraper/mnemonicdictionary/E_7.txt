      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) an exaggerated opinion of your own importance

      <h2>egotistical</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characteristic of those having an inflated idea of their own importance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/egotistic">egotistic</a>
