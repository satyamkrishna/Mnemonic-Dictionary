      <h2>amnesty</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pardon (allowed by government to political criminals)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a period during which offenders are exempt from punishment
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a warrant granting release from punishment for an offense
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pardon">pardon</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the formal act of liberating someone
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/free pardon">free pardon</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pardon">pardon</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) grant a pardon to (a group of people)
