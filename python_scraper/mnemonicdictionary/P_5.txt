      <h2>pantomime</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : acting without dialogue; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a performance using gestures and body movements without words
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dumb show">dumb show</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mime">mime</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) act out without words but with gestures and bodily movements only
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mime">mime</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  The acting students mimed eating an apple               </li>
