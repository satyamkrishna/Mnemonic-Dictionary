      <h2>fallacious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : false; based on a fallacy; misleading; N. fallacy: false idea or notion; false reasoning; Ex. popular fallacy; Ex. fallacy of the argument</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) containing or based on a fallacy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unsound">unsound</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  fallacious reasoning               </li>
                              <li>
                  an unsound argument               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) intended to deceive
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deceitful">deceitful</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fraudulent">fraudulent</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  deceitful advertising               </li>
                              <li>
                  fallacious testimony               </li>
                              <li>
                  smooth, shining, and deceitful as thin ice               </li>
                              <li>
                  a fraudulent scheme to escape paying taxes               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) based on an incorrect or misleading notion or information
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  fallacious hope               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>fallible</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : liable to err</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) likely to fail or make errors
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  everyone is fallible to some degree               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) wanting in moral strength, courage, or will; having the attributes of man as opposed to e.g. divine beings
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/frail">frail</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/imperfect">imperfect</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/weak">weak</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I'm only a fallible human               </li>
                              <li>
                  frail humanity               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
