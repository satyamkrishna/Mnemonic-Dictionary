      <h2>peripatetic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : walking about from place to place (to work); moving; Ex. peripatetic school of philosophy</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who walks from place to place
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a follower of Aristotle or an adherent of Aristotelianism
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aristotelean">aristotelean</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aristotelian">aristotelian</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to Aristotle or his philosophy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aristotelean">aristotelean</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aristotelian">aristotelian</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aristotelic">aristotelic</a>

            
             
         </div>

      <h2>peripheral</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : of a periphery; marginal; outer; of minor importance; not central; Ex. peripheral nerve/interest</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (computer science) electronic equipment connected by cable to the CPU of a computer
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/computer peripheral">computer peripheral</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/peripheral device">peripheral device</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  disk drives and printers are important peripherals               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) on or near an edge or constituting an outer boundary; the outer area
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Russia's peripheral provinces               </li>
                              <li>
                  peripheral suburbs               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) related to the key issue but not of central importance
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a peripheral interest               </li>
                              <li>
                  energy is far from a peripheral issue in the economy               </li>
                              <li>
                  peripheral issues               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) the outside boundary or surface of something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
