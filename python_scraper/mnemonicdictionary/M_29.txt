      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a river in southwestern Alabama; flows into Mobile Bay

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) sculpture suspended in midair whose delicately balanced parts can be set in motion by air currents

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) migratory
