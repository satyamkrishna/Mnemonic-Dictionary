      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) transmitting light; able to be seen through with clarity
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/crystal clear">crystal clear</a>

         </div>
