      <h2>appall</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : dismay; shock deeply</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) strike with disgust or revulsion
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/appal">appal</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/offend">offend</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/outrage">outrage</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scandalize">scandalize</a>

         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The scandalous behavior of this married woman shocked her friends               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/alarm">alarm</a>

         </div>
