      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the fifth and highest element after air and earth and fire and water; was believed to be the substance composing all heavenly bodies
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the purest and most concentrated essence of something
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>quirk</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sudden sharp turn or twist; startling twist; caprice; idiosyncrasy; peculiarity of behavior; Ex. by a quirk of fate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a strange attitude or habit
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/crotchet">crotchet</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/oddity">oddity</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/queerness">queerness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/quirkiness">quirkiness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a narrow groove beside a beading
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) twist or curve abruptly
