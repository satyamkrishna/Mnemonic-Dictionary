      <h2>chimerical</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : fantastically improbable; highly unrealistic; N. chimera: unreal fancy; fire-breathing female creature</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being or relating to or like a chimera
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/chimeral">chimeral</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/chimeric">chimeric</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
