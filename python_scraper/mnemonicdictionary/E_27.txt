         (adj) straying from the right course or from accepted standards

      <h2>erratic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : odd; irregular in movement or behavior; unpredictable</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) liable to sudden unpredictable change

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fickle">fickle</a>
