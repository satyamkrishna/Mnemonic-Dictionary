      <h2>gouge</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : overcharge (with high price); extort from; Ex. gouge the public; CF. usury</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an impression in a surface (as made by a blow)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dent">dent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ding">ding</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nick">nick</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) and edge tool with a blade like a trough for cutting channels or grooves
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of gouging
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) force with the thumb
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/force out">force out</a>

            
             
         </div>
