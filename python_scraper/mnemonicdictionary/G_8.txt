      <h2>germ</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : earliest form of an organism; seed or bud; something that may develop into something larger or more important; microbe</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) anything that provides inspiration for later work
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/seed">seed</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/source">source</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a small apparently simple structure (as a fertilized egg) from which new tissue can develop into a complete organism

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a minute life form (especially a disease-causing bacterium); the term is not in technical use
