      <h2>fulminate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : thunder; explode; issue a severe denunciation</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a salt or ester of fulminic acid
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) criticize severely

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rail">rail</a>

      <h2>fulsome</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : disgustingly excessive; offensively flattering; Ex. fulsome praise/expressions of admire</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) unpleasantly and excessively suave or ingratiating in manner or speech
