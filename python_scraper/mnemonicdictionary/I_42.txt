      <h2>insurgent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : rebellious; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who takes part in an armed rebellion against the constituted authority (especially in the hope of improving conditions)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/freedom fighter">freedom fighter</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/insurrectionist">insurrectionist</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rebel">rebel</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a member of an irregular armed force that fights a stronger force by sabotage and harassment
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/guerilla">guerilla</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/guerrilla">guerrilla</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/irregular">irregular</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) in opposition to a civil authority or government
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/seditious">seditious</a>
