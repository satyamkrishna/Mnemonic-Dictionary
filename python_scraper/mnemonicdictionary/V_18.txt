      <h2>vivid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of light or color) bright and distinct; evoking lifelike mental images; Ex. vivid red hair/description</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) evoking lifelike images within the mind
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/graphic">graphic</a>
