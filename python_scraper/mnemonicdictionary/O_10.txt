      <h2>opprobrium</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : infamy; disgrace arising from shameful conduct; vilification(slander); scorn; contempt; Ex. opprobrium hurled against him; ADJ. opprobrious: expressing contempt; shameful or infamous</p>
