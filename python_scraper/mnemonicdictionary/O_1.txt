      <h2>oath</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : solemn promise; blasphemous use of sacred words to express strong feelings; swear-word; Ex. ``For Christ's sake''</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) profane or obscene expression usually of surprise or anger
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/curse">curse</a>

         </div>

            <u>Synonyms</u> :
