      <h2>transcendental</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : going beyond common thought or ideas; impossible to understand by practical experiences or practices; known only by studying thoughts or intuition; OP. empirical; CF. transcendentalism</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) existing outside of or not in accordance with nature
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nonnatural">nonnatural</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/otherworldly">otherworldly</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/preternatural">preternatural</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  find transcendental motives for sublunary action               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or characteristic of a system of philosophy emphasizing the intuitive and spiritual above the empirical and material
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>transcribe</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : copy; write a copy of; N. transcription</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) write out from speech, notes, etc.
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Transcribe the oral history of this tribe               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) rewrite in a different script
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/transliterate">transliterate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The Sanskrit text had to be transliterated               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) rewrite or arrange a piece of music for an instrument or medium other than that originally intended
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make a phonetic transcription of
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The anthropologist transcribed the sentences of the native informant               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) convert the genetic information in (a strand of DNA) into a strand of RNA, especially messenger RNA
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>transgression</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : violation of a law; sin; V. transgress: go beyond (a limit); violate; do wrong</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of transgressing; the violation of a law or a duty or moral principle
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/evildoing">evildoing</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  the boy was punished for the transgressions of his father               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the spreading of the sea over land as evidenced by the deposition of marine strata over terrestrial strata
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the action of going beyond or overstepping some boundary or limit
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
