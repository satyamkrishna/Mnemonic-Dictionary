      <h2>conventional</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : ordinary; typical; not nuclear; Ex. conventional weapons</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) following accepted customs and proprieties
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  conventional wisdom               </li>
                              <li>
                  she had strayed from the path of conventional behavior               </li>
                              <li>
                  conventional forms of address               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) conforming with accepted standards
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/established">established</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a conventional view of the world               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (weapons) using energy for propulsion or destruction that is not nuclear energy
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  conventional warfare               </li>
                              <li>
                  conventional weapons               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) unimaginative and conformist
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  conventional bourgeois lives               </li>
                              <li>
                  conventional attitudes               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) represented in simplified or symbolic form
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/formal">formal</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/schematic">schematic</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) in accord with or being a tradition or practice accepted from the past
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a conventional church wedding with the bride in traditional white               </li>
                              <li>
                  the conventional handshake               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) rigidly formal or bound by convention
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ceremonious">ceremonious</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  their ceremonious greetings did not seem heartfelt               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>converge</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : approach; tend to meet; come together</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) be adjacent or come together
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/meet">meet</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The lines converge at this point               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) approach a limit as the number of terms increases without limit
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move or draw together at a certain location
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The crowd converged on the movie star               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) come together so as to form a single product
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Social forces converged to bring the Fascists back to power               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>convert</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : one who has adopted a different religion or opinion; V: change into another form; (persuade to) adopt a particular religion or belief</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a person who has been converted to another religious or political belief
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) change from one system to another or to a new plan or policy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

            <ul>
