      <h2>disband</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : dissolve; disperse; (of a group) break up and separate; Ex. The club has disbanded.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to break up or cease to function
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the principal disbanded the political student organization               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>discernible</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : distinguishable; perceivable; Ex. discernible improvement</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) perceptible by the senses or intellect
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/discernable">discernable</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  things happen in the earth and sky with no discernible cause               </li>
                              <li>
                  the newspaper reports no discernible progress in the negotiations               </li>
                              <li>
                  the skyline is easily discernible even at a distance of several miles               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
