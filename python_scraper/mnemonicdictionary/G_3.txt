      <h2>gambit</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : opening in chess in which a piece is sacrificed; action made to produce a future advantage</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an opening remark intended to secure an advantage for the speaker
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ploy">ploy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a maneuver in a game or conversation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ploy">ploy</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />
