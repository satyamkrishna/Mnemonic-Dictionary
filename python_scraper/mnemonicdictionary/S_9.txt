      <h2>scintillate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sparkle; flash; be animated; be full of life; Ex. scintillating conversation</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) give off
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the substance scintillated sparks and flashes               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) reflect brightly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/coruscate">coruscate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sparkle">sparkle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Unquarried marble sparkled on the hillside               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) emit or reflect light in a flickering manner
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twinkle">twinkle</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/winkle">winkle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Does a constellation twinkle more brightly than a single star?               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) physics: fluoresce momentarily when struck by a charged particle or high-energy photon
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the phosphor fluoresced               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) be lively or brilliant or exhibit virtuosity
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/coruscate">coruscate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sparkle">sparkle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The musical performance sparkled               </li>

                  A scintillating conversation               </li>
