      <h2>margin</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : border; room; allowance beyond what is needed; Ex. margin of safety</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the boundary line or the area immediately inside the boundary
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/border">border</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/perimeter">perimeter</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an amount beyond the minimum necessary
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the margin of victory               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the amount of collateral a customer deposits with a broker when borrowing from the broker to buy securities
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/security deposit">security deposit</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (finance) the net sales minus the cost of goods and services sold
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
