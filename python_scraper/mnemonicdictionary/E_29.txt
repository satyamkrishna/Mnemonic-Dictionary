      <h2>ethereal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : like a spirit or fairy; unearthly light; heavenly; unusually refined; Ex. She has an ethereal beauty; CF. ether: upper air</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by lightness and insubstantiality; as impalpable or intangible as air
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aerial">aerial</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aeriform">aeriform</a>

                        ,

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/airy">airy</a>

            
             
         </div>

                  aerial fancies               </li>
