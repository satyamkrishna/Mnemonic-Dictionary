      <h2>lassitude</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : languor; weariness; listlessness</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) a state of comatose torpor (as found in sleeping sickness)

         (noun) a feeling of lack of interest or energy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/languor">languor</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/listlessness">listlessness</a>

            
             
         </div>
