      <h2>plump</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pleasantly fat; nicely rounded</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the sound of a sudden heavy fall
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) drop sharply

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/plummet">plummet</a>

            
             
         </div>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>poise</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : good judgment with composure; balance; V: place in a carefully balanced position</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a cgs unit of dynamic viscosity equal to one dyne-second per square centimeter; the viscosity of a fluid in which a force of one dyne per square centimeter maintains a velocity of 1 centimeter per second
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a state of being balanced in a stable equilibrium
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) great coolness and composure under strain
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/aplomb">aplomb</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/assuredness">assuredness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cool">cool</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sang-froid">sang-froid</a>

            
             
         </div>

                  keep your cool               </li>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
