      <h2>virtual</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : in essence; existing in essence though not in actual form; for practical purposes; Ex. virtual ruler/space</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being actually such in almost every respect
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/practical">practical</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a practical failure               </li>
                              <li>
                  the once elegant temple lay in virtual ruin               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) existing in essence or effect though not in actual fact
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a virtual dependence on charity               </li>

                  a virtual revolution               </li>
