      <h2>tout</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : promote or publicize (one's goods or service); praise excessively (as a form of advertisement); CF. advertise</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who buys tickets to an event in order to resell them at a profit
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ticket tout">ticket tout</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) someone who advertises for customers in an especially brazen way
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/touter">touter</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) one who sells advice about gambling or speculation (especially at the racetrack)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tipster">tipster</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) advertize in strongly positive terms
