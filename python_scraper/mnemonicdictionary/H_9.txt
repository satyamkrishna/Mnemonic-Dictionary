      <h2>herd</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : group of animals; crowd; one who looks after a herd; Ex. shepherd/goatherd; Ex. herd instinct; Ex. herdsman; V: come together in a herd; look after a herd; Ex. herd cattle</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a group of cattle or sheep or other domestic mammals all of the same kind that are herded by humans
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a group of wild mammals of one species that remain together: antelope or elephants or seals or whales or zebra
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a crowd especially of ordinary or undistinguished persons or things
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ruck">ruck</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his brilliance raised him above the ruck               </li>
                              <li>
                  the children resembled a fairy herd               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to herd, drive, or crowd together
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/crowd">crowd</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  We herded the children into a spare classroom               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move together, like a herd
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) keep, move, or drive animals
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Who will be herding the cattle when the cowboy dies?               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

      <h2>hereafter</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : life after death</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) life after death
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/afterlife">afterlife</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the time yet to come
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/future">future</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/futurity">futurity</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/time to come">time to come</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adv) in a subsequent part of this document or statement or matter etc.
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hereinafter">hereinafter</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hereunder">hereunder</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the landlord demises unto the tenant the premises hereinafter called the demised premises               </li>
                              <li>
                  the terms specified hereunder               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adv) in a future life or state
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  hope to win salvation hereafter               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adv) following this in time or order or place; after this
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  hereafter you will no longer receive an allowance               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>heresy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : opinion contrary to popular belief; opinion contrary to accepted religion; ADJ. heretical; CF. heretic</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) any opinions or doctrines at variance with the official or orthodox position
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/heterodoxy">heterodoxy</a>

         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a belief that rejects the orthodox tenets of a religion
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/unorthodoxy">unorthodoxy</a>
