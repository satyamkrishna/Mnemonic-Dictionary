      <h2>vacillate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : waver (in opinion); fluctuate; sway to and fro; N. vacillation</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) be undecided about something; waver between conflicting positions or courses of action
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hover">hover</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/oscillate">oscillate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vibrate">vibrate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He oscillates between accepting the new position and retirement               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move or sway in a rising and falling or wavelike pattern
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fluctuate">fluctuate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/waver">waver</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  the line on the monitor vacillated               </li>

      <h2>vacuous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : empty; lacking in ideas; inane; stupid; Ex. vacuous expression; N. vacuity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) devoid of intelligence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/asinine">asinine</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/fatuous">fatuous</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/inane">inane</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mindless">mindless</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) devoid of significance or point
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/empty">empty</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hollow">hollow</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  a hollow victory               </li>

                  vacuous comments               </li>

      <h2>vain</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : full of self-admiration; conceited; without result; unsuccessful; N. vanity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characteristic of false pride; having an exaggerated sense of self-importance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :

         </div>
