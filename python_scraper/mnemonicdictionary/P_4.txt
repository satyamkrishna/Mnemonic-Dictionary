         (verb) cause to throb or beat rapidly
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>paltry</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : insignificant; petty; trifling; contemptible; Ex. paltry sum; CF. trash</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not worth considering
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/negligible">negligible</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trifling">trifling</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he considered the prize too paltry for the lives it must cost               </li>
                              <li>
                  piffling efforts               </li>
                              <li>
                  a trifling matter               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) contemptibly small in amount
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/measly">measly</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/miserable">miserable</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a measly tip               </li>
                              <li>
                  the company donated a miserable $100 for flood relief               </li>
                              <li>
                  a paltry wage               </li>
                              <li>
                  almost depleted his miserable store of dried beans               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>pandemic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : widespread; affecting the majority of people; N: pandemic disease; CF. all people</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an epidemic that is geographically widespread; occurring throughout a region or even throughout the world
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) epidemic over a wide geographical area
