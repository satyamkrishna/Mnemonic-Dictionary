      <h2>sleeper</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : one that achieves unexpected success; something originally of little value or importance that in time becomes very valuable</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a rester who is sleeping
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/slumberer">slumberer</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a spy or saboteur or terrorist planted in an enemy country who lives there as a law-abiding citizen until activated by a prearranged signal
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an unexpected achiever of success
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

      <h2>slight</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : insult to one's dignity; snub; V: treat as if of small importance; insult; snub; ADJ. small in size; of small importance</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a deliberate discourteous act (usually as an expression of anger or disapproval)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
