      <h2>analogy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : similarity; parallelism; comparing two similar things</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an inference that if things agree in some respects they probably agree in others
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) drawing a comparison in order to show a similarity in some respect
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the operation of a computer presents and interesting analogy to the working of the brain               </li>
                              <li>
