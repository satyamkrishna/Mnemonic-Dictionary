      <h2>bourgeois</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : middle class; selfishly materialistic; too interested in material possessions</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a capitalist who engages in industrial commercial enterprise
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/businessperson">businessperson</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a member of the middle class
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/burgher">burgher</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (according to Marxist thought) being of the property-owning class and exploitive of the working class
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) conforming to the standards and conventions of the middle class
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conservative">conservative</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/materialistic">materialistic</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a bourgeois mentality               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) belonging to the middle class
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>bout</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : match; short period of great activity; Ex. wrestling bout; bout of drinking/flu</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (sports) a division during which one team is on the offensive
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/round">round</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/turn">turn</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a period of illness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a bout of fever               </li>
                              <li>
                  a bout of depression               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a contest or fight (especially between boxers or wrestlers)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an occasion for excessive eating or drinking
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/binge">binge</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bust">bust</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tear">tear</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  they went on a bust that lasted three days               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
