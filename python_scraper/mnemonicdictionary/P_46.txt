      <h2>preclude</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make impossible; prevent; exclude; eliminate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) keep from happening or arising; make impossible
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forbid">forbid</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/foreclose">foreclose</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forestall">forestall</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prevent">prevent</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  My sense of tact forbids an honest answer               </li>
                              <li>
                  Your role in the projects precludes your involvement in the competitive project               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />

      <h2>precocious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : advanced in development; N. precocity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by or characteristic of exceptionally early development or maturity (especially in mental aptitude)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a precocious child               </li>
                              <li>
                  a precocious achievement               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) appearing or developing early
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  precocious flowers appear before the leaves as in some species of magnolias               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
