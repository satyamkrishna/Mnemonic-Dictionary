      <h2>incline</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : slope; slant; Ex. steep incline</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an elevated geological formation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/side">side</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/slope">slope</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he climbed the steep slope               </li>
                              <li>
                  the house was built on the side of a mountain               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an inclined surface connecting two levels
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ramp">ramp</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) have a tendency or disposition to do or be something; be inclined
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/be given">be given</a>

         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She tends to be nervous before her lectures               </li>
                              <li>
                  These dresses run small               </li>
                              <li>
                  He inclined to corpulence               </li>

         (verb) bend or turn (one's ear) towards a speaker in order to listen well

         <u>Definition</u><br />

         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
