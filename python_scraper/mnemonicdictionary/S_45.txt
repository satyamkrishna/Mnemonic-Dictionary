      <h2>spin</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : rotate swiftly; make (thread) by twisting (cotton, wool, etc.); N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a swift whirling motion (usually of a missile)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of rotating rapidly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twirl">twirl</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twist">twist</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twisting">twisting</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/whirl">whirl</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he gave the crank a spin               </li>
                              <li>
                  it broke off after much twisting               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a short drive in a car
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he took the new car for a spin               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) rapid descent of an aircraft in a steep spiral
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tailspin">tailspin</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a distinctive interpretation (especially as used by politicians to sway public opinion)
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the campaign put a favorable spin on the story               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) revolve quickly and repeatedly around one's own axis
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/gyrate">gyrate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reel">reel</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spin around">spin around</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/whirl">whirl</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The dervishes whirl around and around without getting dizzy               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) stream in jets, of liquids
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The creek spun its course through the woods               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to spin
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/birl">birl</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twirl">twirl</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/whirl">whirl</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spin a coin               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make up a story
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spin a yarn               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) form a web by making a thread
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spiders spin a fine web               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) work natural fibers into a thread
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spin silk               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) twist and turn so as to give an intended interpretation
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The President's spokesmen had to spin the story to make it less embarrassing               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) prolong or extend
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
