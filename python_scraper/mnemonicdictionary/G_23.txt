      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : complain; mutter discontentedly; grouch; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a loud low dull continuous noise
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a complaint uttered in a low and indistinct tone
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grumbling">grumbling</a>

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/grouch">grouch</a>
