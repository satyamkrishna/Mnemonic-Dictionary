         (noun) precipitation of ice pellets when there are strong rising air currents
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) many objects thrown forcefully through the air

      <h2>halcyon</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : calm; peaceful; Ex. halcyon days</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (Greek mythology) a woman who was turned into a kingfisher
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/alcyone">alcyone</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a large kingfisher widely distributed in warmer parts of the Old World
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/genus halcyon">genus halcyon</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a mythical bird said to breed at the time of the winter solstice in a nest floating on the sea and to have the power of calming the winds and waves
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) idyllically calm and peaceful; suggesting happy tranquillity
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a halcyon atmosphere               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by peace and prosperity
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/golden">golden</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prosperous">prosperous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a golden era               </li>
                              <li>
                  the halcyon days of the clipper trade               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>hallucination</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : delusion; false idea; false perception of objects with a compelling sense of their reality; objects so perceived; V. hallucinate; ADJ. hallucinatory</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) illusory perception; a common symptom of severe mental disorder
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a mistaken or unfounded opinion or idea
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/delusion">delusion</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he has delusions of competence               </li>
                              <li>
                  his dreams of vast wealth are a hallucination               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an object perceived during a hallucinatory episode
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he refused to believe that the angel was a hallucination               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
