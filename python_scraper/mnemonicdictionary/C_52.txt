      <h2>conjecture</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : surmise; guess; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a hypothesis that has been formed by speculating or conjecturing (usually with little hard evidence)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/speculation">speculation</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  speculations about the outcome of the election               </li>
                              <li>
                  he dismissed it as mere conjecture               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a message expressing an opinion based on incomplete evidence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/guess">guess</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hypothesis">hypothesis</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/speculation">speculation</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/supposition">supposition</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/surmisal">surmisal</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/surmise">surmise</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) reasoning that involves the formation of conclusions from incomplete evidence
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) to believe especially on uncertain or tentative grounds
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hypothecate">hypothecate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hypothesise">hypothesise</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hypothesize">hypothesize</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/speculate">speculate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/suppose">suppose</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/theorise">theorise</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/theorize">theorize</a>

            
             
         </div>
