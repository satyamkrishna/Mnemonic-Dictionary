      <h2>rejuvenate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make young again</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause (a stream or river) to erode, as by an uplift of the land
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) develop youthful topographical features
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the land rejuvenated               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The contact with his grandchildren rejuvenated him               </li>
                           </ul>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
