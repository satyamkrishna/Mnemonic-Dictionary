      <h2>reciprocal</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : mutual; given and received in return; exchangeable; interacting; Ex. reciprocal trade agreement</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something (a term or expression or concept) that has a reciprocal relation to something else
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  risk is the reciprocal of safety               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (mathematics) one of a pair of numbers whose product is 1: the reciprocal of 2/3 is 3/2; the multiplicative inverse of 7 is 1/7
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/multiplicative inverse">multiplicative inverse</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) hybridization involving a pair of crosses that reverse the sexes associated with each genotype
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reciprocal cross">reciprocal cross</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) concerning each of two or more persons or things; especially given or done in return
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mutual">mutual</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  reciprocal aid               </li>
                              <li>
                  reciprocal trade               </li>
                              <li>
                  mutual respect               </li>
                              <li>
                  reciprocal privileges at other clubs               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to the multiplicative inverse of a quantity or function
