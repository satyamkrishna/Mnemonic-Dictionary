      <h2>dogmatic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : opinionated; holding stubbornly to one's opinion; arbitrary; doctrinal</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by assertion of unproved or unprovable principles
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dogmatical">dogmatical</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or pertaining to or characteristic of a doctrine or code of beliefs accepted as authoritative
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) relating to or involving dogma

      <h2>doldrums</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : blues; listlessness(lack of spirit or energy); slack(inactive) period; period of stagnation; ocean area near the equator where ships cannot move because there is no wind; Ex. in the doldrums</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a state of inactivity (in business or art etc)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stagnancy">stagnancy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stagnation">stagnation</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  economic growth of less than 1% per year is considered to be economic stagnation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a belt of calms and light winds between the northern and southern trade winds of the Atlantic and Pacific
