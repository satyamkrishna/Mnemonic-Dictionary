      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) driving or forcing

      <h2>compilation</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : listing of information in tabular or book form; compiling</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something that is compiled (as into a single book or file)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/digest">digest</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of compiling (as into a single book or file or list)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/compiling">compiling</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the job of compiling the inventory took several hours               </li>
