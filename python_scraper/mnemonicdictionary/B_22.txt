      <h2>bog</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : soft wet land; V: hinder or be hindered; Ex. bogged down in the mud</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) wet spongy ground of decomposing vegetation; has poorer drainage than a swamp; soil is unfit for cultivation but can be cut and dried and used for fuel
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/peat bog">peat bog</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to slow down or get stuck
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bog down">bog down</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

         <u>Definition</u><br />

            <u>Synonyms</u> :

      <h2>Bohemian</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : unconventional (in an artistic way)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a member of a people with dark skin and hair who speak Romany and who traditionally live by seasonal work and fortunetelling; they are believed to have originated in northern India but now are living on all continents (but mostly in Europe, North Africa, and North America)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/gipsy">gipsy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/gypsy">gypsy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/roma">roma</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/romani">romani</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/romany">romany</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rommany">rommany</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a native or inhabitant of Bohemia in the Czech Republic
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a nonconformist writer or artist who lives an unconventional life
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or relating to Bohemia or its language or people
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) unconventional in especially appearance and behavior

                  a bohemian life style               </li>
