      <h2>extemporaneous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not planned; impromptu; extempore</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) with little or no preparation or forethought
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ad-lib">ad-lib</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/extempore">extempore</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/impromptu">impromptu</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/offhanded">offhanded</a>

      <h2>extirpate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : root up; uproot; destroy completely</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) destroy completely, as if down to the roots
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/eradicate">eradicate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/exterminate">exterminate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/root out">root out</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/uproot">uproot</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the vestiges of political democracy were soon uprooted               </li>
                              <li>
                  root out corruption               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) pull up by or as if by the roots
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deracinate">deracinate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/root out">root out</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/uproot">uproot</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  uproot the vine that has spread all over the garden               </li>
