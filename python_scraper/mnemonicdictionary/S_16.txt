      <h2>seine</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : seine net; net for catching fish</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a French river that flows through the heart of Paris and then northward into the English Channel
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/seine river">seine river</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a large fishnet that hangs vertically, with floats at the top and weights at the bottom
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) fish with a seine; catch fish with a seine
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
