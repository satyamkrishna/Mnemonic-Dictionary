      <h2>cue</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : word or signal (as in a play to prompt another actor's speech or entrance); reminder or hint; V: give a cue to</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an actor's line that immediately precedes and serves as a reminder for some action or speech
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) evidence that helps to solve a problem
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clew">clew</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clue">clue</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a stimulus that provides information about what to do
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/discriminative stimulus">discriminative stimulus</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
