      <h2>deciduous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : falling off at a specific season or stage of growth as of leaves; Ex. deciduous tree/teeth</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (of plants and shrubs) shedding foliage at the end of the growing season
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (of teeth, antlers, etc.) being shed at the end of a period of growth
