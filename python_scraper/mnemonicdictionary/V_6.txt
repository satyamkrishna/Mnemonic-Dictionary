      <h2>venial</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of a fault or sin) forgivable; trivial</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) warranting only temporal punishment
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
