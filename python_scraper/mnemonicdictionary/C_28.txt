      <h2>clangor</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : loud resounding noise; sound of repeated clanging</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a loud resonant repeating noise
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clang">clang</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clangoring">clangoring</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clangour">clangour</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/clash">clash</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/crash">crash</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                           </ul>
