      <h2>genre</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : particular variety of art or literature</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a kind of literary or artistic work
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a style of expressing yourself in writing
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/literary genre">literary genre</a>

         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an expressive style of music
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/music genre">music genre</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/musical genre">musical genre</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/musical style">musical style</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a class of art (or artistic endeavor) having a characteristic form or technique
