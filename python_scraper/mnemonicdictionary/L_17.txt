      <h2>loom</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : appear or take shape (usually in an enlarged, indistinct, or distorted form); Ex. The shadow of the gallows loomed threateningly. N: apparatus for making thread into cloth</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a textile machine for weaving yarn into a textile
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) come into view indistinctly, often threateningly
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Another air plane loomed into the sky               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) appear very large or occupy a commanding position
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hulk">hulk</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/predominate">predominate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tower">tower</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The huge sculpture predominates over the fountain               </li>
                              <li>
                  Large shadows loomed on the canyon wall               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) hang over, as of something threatening, dark, or menacing
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/brood">brood</a>
