      <h2>rare</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of air) thin in density; rarefied; Ex. rare air of the mountains</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not widely known; especially valued for its uncommonness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a rare word               </li>
                              <li>
                  rare books               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) recurring only at long intervals
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a rare appearance               </li>
                              <li>
                  total eclipses are rare events               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not widely distributed
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  rare herbs               </li>
                              <li>
                  rare patches of green in the desert               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having low density
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rarefied">rarefied</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rarified">rarified</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  rare gasses               </li>
                              <li>
                  lightheaded from the rarefied mountain air               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by an uncommon quality; especially superlative or extreme of its kind
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/uncommon">uncommon</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  what is so rare as a day in June               </li>
                              <li>
                  a rare skill               </li>
                              <li>
                  an uncommon sense of humor               </li>
                              <li>
                  she was kind to an uncommon degree               </li>
