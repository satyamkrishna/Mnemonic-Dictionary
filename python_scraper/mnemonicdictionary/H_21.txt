      <h2>hypothetical</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : based on assumptions or hypotheses; supposed; N. hypothesis</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a hypothetical possibility, circumstance, statement, proposal, situation, etc.
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  consider the following, just as a hypothetical               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) based primarily on surmise rather than adequate evidence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/conjectural">conjectural</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/divinatory">divinatory</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hypothetic">hypothetic</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/supposed">supposed</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/suppositional">suppositional</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/suppositious">suppositious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/supposititious">supposititious</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  theories about the extinction of dinosaurs are still highly conjectural               </li>

                  the supposed reason for his absence               </li>
                              <li>
