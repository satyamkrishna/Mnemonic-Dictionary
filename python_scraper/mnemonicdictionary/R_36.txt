      <h2>retract</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : withdraw; take back; draw back; Ex. retract a statement/an offer/claws; N. retraction; CF. retractile</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) formally reject or disavow a formerly held belief, usually under pressure
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abjure">abjure</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forswear">forswear</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/recant">recant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resile">resile</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He retracted his earlier statements about his religion               </li>
                              <li>
                  She abjured her beliefs               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) pull away from a source of disgust or fear
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shrink back">shrink back</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) use a surgical instrument to hold open (the edges of a wound or an organ)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/draw back">draw back</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pull back">pull back</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) pull inward or towards a center
