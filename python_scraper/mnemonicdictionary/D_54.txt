      <h2>dull</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of colors or surfaces) not bright; cloudy; overcast; boring; (of edge or sound) not sharp; not rapid; sluggish; slow in thinking and understanding; stupid; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make dull in appearance
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Age had dulled the surface               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) become dull or lusterless in appearance; lose shine or brightness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the varnished table top dulled with time               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) deaden (a sound or noise), especially by wrapping
