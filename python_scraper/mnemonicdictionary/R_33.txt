      <h2>respiration</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : breathing; exhalation; ADJ. respiratory</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the metabolic processes whereby certain organisms obtain energy from organic molecules; processes that take place in the cells and tissues during which energy is released and carbon dioxide is produced and absorbed by the blood to be transported to the lungs
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cellular respiration">cellular respiration</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/internal respiration">internal respiration</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a single complete act of breathing in and out
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  thirty respirations per minute               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the bodily process of inhalation and exhalation; the process of taking in oxygen from inhaled air and releasing carbon dioxide by exhalation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/breathing">breathing</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/external respiration">external respiration</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ventilation">ventilation</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>respite</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : time for rest; interval of relief; delay in punishment; reprieve</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a (temporary) relief from harm or discomfort
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reprieve">reprieve</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a pause from doing something (as work)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/break">break</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/recess">recess</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/time out">time out</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  we took a 10-minute break               </li>
                              <li>
                  he took time out to recuperate               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an interruption in the intensity or amount of something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abatement">abatement</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hiatus">hiatus</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reprieve">reprieve</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/suspension">suspension</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a pause for relaxation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/relief">relief</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rest">rest</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rest period">rest period</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  people actually accomplish more when they take time for short rests               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of reprieving; postponing or remitting punishment
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reprieve">reprieve</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) postpone the punishment of a convicted criminal, such as an execution

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) a sum of money paid in compensation for loss or injury
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/amends">amends</a>
