      <h2>hazard</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : venture; put in danger; risk; Ex. hazard a guess; N: possible source of danger</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a source of danger; a possibility of incurring loss or misfortune

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/endangerment">endangerment</a>
