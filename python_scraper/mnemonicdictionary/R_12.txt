         (verb) act, feel, or give mutually or in return
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>recital</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : act of reciting publicly; detailed account; performance of music or dance (by a solo performer)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of giving an account describing incidents or a course of events
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/narration">narration</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/yarn">yarn</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his narration was hesitant               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) performance of music or dance especially by soloists
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a public instance of reciting or repeating (from memory) something prepared in advance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/reading">reading</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/recitation">recitation</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the program included songs and recitations of well-loved poems               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a detailed statement giving facts and figures
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his wife gave a recital of his infidelities               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a detailed account or description of something
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
