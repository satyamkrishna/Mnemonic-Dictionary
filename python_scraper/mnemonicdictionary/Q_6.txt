      <h2>quizzical</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : curious; suggesting puzzlement (without saying); questioning; teasing; mocking; bantering; Ex. quizzical glance</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) playfully vexing (especially by ridicule)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mocking">mocking</a>

         </div>

      <h2>quotidian</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : daily; commonplace; customary; Ex. quotidian routine</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) found in the ordinary course of events
