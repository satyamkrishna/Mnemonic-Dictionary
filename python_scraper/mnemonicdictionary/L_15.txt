      <h2>litter</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : waste material thrown away (as bits of paper scattered untidily); V: cover untidily with scattered litter</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         (noun) the offspring at one birth of a multiparous mammal
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) rubbish carelessly dropped or left about (especially in public places)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) conveyance consisting of a chair or bed carried on two poles by bearers
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) material used to provide a bed for animals
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bedding">bedding</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bedding material">bedding material</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) strew

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
