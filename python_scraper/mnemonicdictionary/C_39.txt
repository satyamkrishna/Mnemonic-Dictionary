         (noun) an object (such as a coin or postage stamp) made to mark an event or honor a person
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) intended as a commemoration

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
