      <h2>exclaim</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : cry out suddenly; N. exclamation; ADJ. exclamatory</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) utter aloud; often with surprise, horror, or joy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :

         </div>
