      <h2>discretion</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : prudence; ability to adjust actions to circumstances; freedom of action or judgment; ADJ. discreet; CF. discretionary</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) freedom to act or judge on one's own
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) knowing how to avoid embarrassment or distress
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/circumspection">circumspection</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/discreetness">discreetness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/prudence">prudence</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the servants showed great tact and discretion               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) refined taste; tact
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/delicacy">delicacy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>discriminate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : distinguish; make distinctions on the basis of preference</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) recognize or perceive the difference
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/know apart">know apart</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) treat differently on the basis of sex or race
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/separate">separate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/single out">single out</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) distinguish
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I could not discriminate the different tastes in this complicated dish               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by the ability to see or make fine distinctions
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  discriminate judgments               </li>
