      <h2>imponderable</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : weightless; that cannot undergo precise evaluation; CF. pound</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a factor whose effects cannot be accurately assessed
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  human behavior depends on many imponderables               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) difficult or impossible to evaluate with precision
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>

                           </ul>

      <h2>import</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : significance; importance; meaning</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) commodities (goods or services) bought from a foreign country
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/importation">importation</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an imported person brought from a foreign country
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/importee">importee</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the lead role was played by an import from Sweden               </li>
                              <li>
                  they are descendants of indentured importees               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the message that is intended or expressed or signified
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/meaning">meaning</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/significance">significance</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/signification">signification</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  what is the meaning of this sentence               </li>
                              <li>
                  the significance of a red traffic light               </li>
                              <li>
                  the signification of Chinese characters               </li>
                              <li>
                  the import of his announcement was ambiguous               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a meaning that is not expressly stated but can be inferred
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/implication">implication</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/significance">significance</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the significance of his remark became clear only later               </li>
                              <li>
                  the expectation was spread both by word and by implication               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) having important effects or influence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/consequence">consequence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/moment">moment</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  decisions of great consequence are made by the president himself               </li>
                              <li>
                  virtue is of more moment than security               </li>
                              <li>
                  that result is of no consequence               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) bring in from abroad

         <u>Definition</u><br />
