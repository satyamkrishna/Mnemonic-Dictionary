      <h2>makeshift</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : temporary expedient or substitute (in the case of urgent need); Ex. makeshift shelter</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something contrived to meet an urgent need or emergency
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/make-do">make-do</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stopgap">stopgap</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) done or made using whatever is available
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/improvised">improvised</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/jury-rigged">jury-rigged</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  crossed the river on improvised bridges               </li>
