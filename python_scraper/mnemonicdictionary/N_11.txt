      <h2>nullify</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : make invalid; make null; invalidate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) declare invalid
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/annul">annul</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/avoid">avoid</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/invalidate">invalidate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/quash">quash</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/void">void</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The contract was annulled               </li>
                              <li>
                  void a plea               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) show to be invalid
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/invalidate">invalidate</a>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make ineffective by counterbalancing the effect of
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/negate">negate</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/neutralise">neutralise</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/neutralize">neutralize</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Her optimism neutralizes his gloom               </li>
                              <li>
                  This action will negate the effect of my efforts               </li>
