      <h2>veto</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : vested power to reject a bill; exercise of this right; V: prevent or forbid by exercising the power of veto</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a vote that blocks a decision
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the power or right to prohibit or reject a proposed or intended act (especially the power of a chief executive to reject a bill passed by the legislature)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) vote against; refuse to endorse; refuse to assent
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/blackball">blackball</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/negative">negative</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The President vetoed the bill               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) command against
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
