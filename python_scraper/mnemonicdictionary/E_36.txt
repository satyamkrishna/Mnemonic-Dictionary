      <h2>exempt</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not subject to a duty or obligation; free from a duty; V.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) grant relief or an exemption from a rule or requirement to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/free">free</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/relieve">relieve</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  She exempted me from the exam               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) grant exemption or release to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/excuse">excuse</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/let off">let off</a>

         </div>

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (of persons) freed from or not subject to an obligation or liability (as e.g. taxes) to which others or other things are subject
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a beauty somehow exempt from the aging process               </li>

                  exempt from jury duty               </li>
                              <li>
                  only the very poorest citizens should be exempt from income taxes               </li>
                           </ul>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (of goods or funds) not subject to taxation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nontaxable">nontaxable</a>

            
             
         </div>
