      <h2>pedantic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : bookish; showing off learning; marked by an excessive ostentatious concern for book learning; N. pedantry</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by a narrow focus on or display of learning especially its trivial aspects
