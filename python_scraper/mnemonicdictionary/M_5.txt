      <h2>malign</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : speak evil of; bad-mouth(criticize spitefully); defame; ADJ: harmful; Ex. malign influence</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) speak unfavorably about
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/badmouth">badmouth</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/drag through the mud">drag through the mud</a>

         </div>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) evil or harmful in nature or influence
