      <h2>wane</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : decrease in size or strength (after being full); grow gradually to an end; Ex. The moon waxes and wanes every month; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a gradual decline (in size or strength or power or number)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ebb">ebb</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ebbing">ebbing</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) grow smaller
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/decline">decline</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/go down">go down</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  Interest in the project waned               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) become smaller

      <h2>wangle</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : achieve by cleverness or trick; wiggle out; fake; Ex. She tried to wangle an invitation to the party.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an instance of accomplishing something by scheming or trickery
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :

      <h2>warp</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : twist out of shape; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a twist or aberration; especially a perverse or abnormal way of judging or acting
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/deflection">deflection</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a shape distorted by twisting or folding
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/buckle">buckle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a moral or mental distortion
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/warping">warping</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) yarn arranged lengthways on a loom and crossed by the woof
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make false by mutilation or addition; as of a message or story
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/distort">distort</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/falsify">falsify</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/garble">garble</a>

            
             
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) bend out of shape, as under pressure or from heat
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/buckle">buckle</a>
