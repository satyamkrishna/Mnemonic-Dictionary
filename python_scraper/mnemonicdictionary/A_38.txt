      <h2>antiseptic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : substance that prevents infection in a wound; ADJ.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a substance that destroys micro-organisms that carry disease without harming body tissues
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) thoroughly clean and free of or destructive to disease-causing organisms
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  doctors in antiseptic green coats               </li>
                              <li>
                  the antiseptic effect of alcohol               </li>
                              <li>
                  it is said that marjoram has antiseptic qualities               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) clean and honest
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  antiseptic financial practices               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) freeing from error or corruption
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the antiseptic effect of sturdy criticism               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) devoid of objectionable language
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  lyrics as antiseptic as Sunday School               </li>
