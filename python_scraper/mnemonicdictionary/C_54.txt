      <h2>consecrate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : dedicate; sanctify; declare as sacred; Ex. consecrate one's life to helping the poor</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) appoint to a clerical posts
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ordain">ordain</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/order">order</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ordinate">ordinate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he was ordained in the Church               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) give entirely to a specific person, activity, or cause
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/commit">commit</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dedicate">dedicate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/devote">devote</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/give">give</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She committed herself to the work of God               </li>
                              <li>
                  give one's talents to a good cause               </li>
                              <li>
                  consecrate your life to the church               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) dedicate to a deity by a vow
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/vow">vow</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) render holy by means of religious rites
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bless">bless</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hallow">hallow</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sanctify">sanctify</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) solemnly dedicated to or set apart for a high purpose
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/consecrated">consecrated</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dedicated">dedicated</a>

         </div>
