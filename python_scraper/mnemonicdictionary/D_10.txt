      <h2>deference</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : courteous regard for another's wish; courteous yielding to another's wish or opinion (showing respect); ADJ. deferential; OP. effrontery</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a courteous expression (by word or deed) of esteem or regard
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/respect">respect</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his deference to her wishes was very flattering               </li>
                              <li>
                  be sure to give my respects to the dean               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) courteous regard for people's feelings
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/respect">respect</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/respectfulness">respectfulness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  in deference to your wishes               </li>
                              <li>
                  out of respect for his privacy               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a disposition or tendency to yield to the will of others
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/complaisance">complaisance</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/compliance">compliance</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/compliancy">compliancy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/obligingness">obligingness</a>
