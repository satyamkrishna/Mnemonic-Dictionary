      <h2>attest</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : testify; bear witness</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) provide evidence for; stand as proof of; show by one's behavior, attitude, or external attributes
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/certify">certify</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/demonstrate">demonstrate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/evidence">evidence</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/manifest">manifest</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  His high fever attested to his illness               </li>
                              <li>
                  The buildings in Rome manifest a high level of architectural sophistication               </li>
                              <li>
                  This decision demonstrates his sense of fairness               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) authenticate, affirm to be true, genuine, or correct, as in an official capacity
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I attest this signature               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) give testimony in a court of law
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bear witness">bear witness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/take the stand">take the stand</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/testify">testify</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) establish or verify the usage of
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  This word is not attested until 1993               </li>
