      <h2>pretend</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : feign; pretend to: claim to possess; make pretensions to; Ex. I don't pretend to much expertise; N. pretense</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the enactment of a pretense
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/make-believe">make-believe</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  it was just pretend               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make believe with the intent to deceive
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/affect">affect</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dissemble">dissemble</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/feign">feign</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sham">sham</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He feigned that he was ill               </li>
                              <li>
                  He shammed a headache               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) behave unnaturally or affectedly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/act">act</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dissemble">dissemble</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  She's just acting               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) put forward a claim and assert right or possession of
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  pretend the title of King               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) put forward, of a guess, in spite of possible refutation
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/guess">guess</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/hazard">hazard</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/venture">venture</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I am guessing that the price of real estate will rise again               </li>
                              <li>
                  I cannot pretend to say that you are wrong               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) represent fictitiously, as in a play, or pretend to be or act like
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
