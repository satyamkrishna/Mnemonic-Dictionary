      <h2>din</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : continued loud noise; V: make a din; instill by wearying repetition</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a loud harsh or strident noise
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/blare">blare</a>

                        ,

         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of making a noisy disturbance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/commotion">commotion</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make a resonant sound, like artillery

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/boom">boom</a>
