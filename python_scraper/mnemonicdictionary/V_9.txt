      <h2>verge</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : border; edge, rim, or margin; Ex. on the verge of: very near to; V: border on</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a region marking a boundary
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/brink">brink</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/threshold">threshold</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the limit beyond which something happens or changes
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/brink">brink</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  on the verge of tears               </li>
                              <li>
                  on the brink of bankruptcy               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a ceremonial or emblematic staff
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/scepter">scepter</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sceptre">sceptre</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/wand">wand</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a grass border along a road
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) border on; come close to
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
