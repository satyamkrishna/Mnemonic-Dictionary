      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a writer who argues in opposition to others (especially in theology)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/polemicist">polemicist</a>

         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a controversy (especially over a belief or dogma)
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of or involving dispute or controversy
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/polemical">polemical</a>

      <h2>politic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : prudent; judicious; well judged; expedient; well devised</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by artful prudence, expedience, and shrewdness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  it is neither polite nor politic to get into other people's quarrels               </li>
                              <li>
                  a politic decision               </li>
                              <li>
                  a politic manager               </li>
                              <li>
                  a politic old scoundrel               </li>
                              <li>
                  a shrewd and politic reply               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
