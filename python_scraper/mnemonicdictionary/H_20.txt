      <h2>hybrid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : mongrel; mixed breed; V. hybridize</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a word that is composed of parts from different languages (e.g., `monolingual' has a Greek prefix and a Latin root)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/loan-blend">loan-blend</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/loanblend">loanblend</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a composite of mixed origin
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the vice-presidency is a hybrid of administrative and legislative offices               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (genetics) an organism that is the offspring of genetically dissimilar parents or stock; especially offspring produced by breeding plants or animals of different varieties or breeds or species
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cross">cross</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/crossbreed">crossbreed</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a mule is a cross between a horse and a donkey               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) produced by crossbreeding
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
