      <h2>shuffle</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : mix together; jumble; move (something) from one place to another; slide (the feet) along the ground while walking; Ex. shuffle papers from one pile to another; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of mixing cards haphazardly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/make">make</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) walking with a slow dragging motion without lifting your feet
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shamble">shamble</a>

      <h2>shunt</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : move (a railway train) from one track to another; turn aside; divert; sidetrack; Ex. shunt traffic around an accident; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a passage by which a bodily fluid (especially blood) is diverted from one channel to another
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an arteriovenus shunt               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a conductor having low resistance in parallel with another device to divert a fraction of the current
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bypass">bypass</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/electrical shunt">electrical shunt</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) implant consisting of a tube made of plastic or rubber; for draining fluids within the body
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) transfer to another track, of trains
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) provide with or divert by means of an electrical shunt
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
