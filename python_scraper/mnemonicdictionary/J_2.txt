      <h2>jaunty</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : cheerful and pleased with life; lighthearted; animated; easy and carefree; dapper in appearance; Ex. jaunty person/hat</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by up-to-dateness in dress and manners
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dapper">dapper</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dashing">dashing</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/natty">natty</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/raffish">raffish</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rakish">rakish</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/snappy">snappy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spiffy">spiffy</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spruce">spruce</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  a dapper young man               </li>
                              <li>
                  a jaunty red hat               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/chipper">chipper</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/debonair">debonair</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/debonaire">debonaire</a>

            
             
         </div>

                  life that is gay, brisk, and debonair               </li>
