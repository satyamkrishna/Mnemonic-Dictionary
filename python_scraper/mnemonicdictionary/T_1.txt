      <h2>tablet</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : small round piece of medicine; flat piece of stone or metal bearing an inscription; Ex. stone tablet on the wall</p>

         (noun) a slab of stone or wood suitable for bearing an inscription

      <h2>tacit</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : understood (without actually being expressed); not put into words; Ex. tacit agreement</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) implied by or inferred from actions or statements
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/silent">silent</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/understood">understood</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                  gave silent consent               </li>

                  a tacit agreement               </li>
                              <li>
                  the understood provisos of a custody agreement               </li>

      <h2>tactful</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : careful no to cause offence; OP. tactless</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having or showing a sense of what is fitting and considerate in dealing with others
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
