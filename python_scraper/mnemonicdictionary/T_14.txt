      <h2>throb</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : (of a heart or machine) beat rapidly or violently; N. Ex. hearthrob: heartbeat; sweetheart</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a deep pulsating type of pain
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an instance of rapid strong pulsation (of the heart)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pounding">pounding</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/throbbing">throbbing</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he felt a throbbing in his head               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) pulsate or pound with abnormal force
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  my head is throbbing               </li>
                              <li>
                  Her heart was throbbing               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) expand and contract rhythmically; beat rhythmically
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pulsate">pulsate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pulse">pulse</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The baby's heart was pulsating again after the surgeon massaged it               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) tremble convulsively, as from fear or excitement
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shiver">shiver</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/shudder">shudder</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/thrill">thrill</a>
