      <h2>impasse</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : predicament(dangerous condition) from which there is no escape; situation allowing for no further progress</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a situation in which no progress can be made or no advancement is possible
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dead end">dead end</a>

      <h2>impassive</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : without feeling; expressionless; imperturbable; stoical; Ex. impassive face</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having or revealing little emotion or sensibility; not easily aroused or excited
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/stolid">stolid</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  her impassive remoteness               </li>
                              <li>
                  he remained impassive, showing neither interest in nor concern for our plight               </li>
                              <li>
                  a silent stolid creature who took it all as a matter of course               </li>
                              <li>
                  her face showed nothing but stolid indifference               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) deliberately impassive in manner
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
