      <h2>monumental</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : massive; impressively large; built as a monument</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) relating or belonging to or serving as a monument
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the use of the arch in monumental architecture               </li>
                              <li>
                  monumental sculptures               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of outstanding significance
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Einstein's monumental contributions to physics               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) imposing in size or bulk or solidity

      <h2>morbid</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : given to unwholesome or unhealthy thought; moody; characteristic of disease; Ex. morbid curiosity; N. morbidity; CF. disease</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
