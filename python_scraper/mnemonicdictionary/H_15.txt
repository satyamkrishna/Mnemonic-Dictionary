      <h2>homespun</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : domestic; made at home; spun or woven at home; simple and ordinary; Ex. homespun philosophy</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a rough loosely woven fabric originally made with yarn that was spun at home
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of textiles; having a rough surface
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nubbly">nubbly</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nubby">nubby</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/slubbed">slubbed</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tweedy">tweedy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a sweater knitted of nubbly homespun yarns               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characteristic of country life
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cracker-barrel">cracker-barrel</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/folksy">folksy</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  cracker-barrel philosophy               </li>
                              <li>
                  folksy humor               </li>
                              <li>
                  the air of homespun country boys               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) made of cloth spun or woven in the home
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
