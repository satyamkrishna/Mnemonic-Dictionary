      <h2>extraneous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not essential; irrelevant; superfluous; external; coming from the outside; Ex. extraneous details/noise/to the subject</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not pertinent to the matter under consideration
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/immaterial">immaterial</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/impertinent">impertinent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/orthogonal">orthogonal</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an issue extraneous to the debate               </li>
                              <li>
                  the price was immaterial               </li>
                              <li>
                  mentioned several impertinent facts before finally coming to the point               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not essential
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the ballet struck me as extraneous and somewhat out of keeping with the rest of the play               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not belonging to that in which it is contained; introduced from an outside source
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/foreign">foreign</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  water free of extraneous matter               </li>
                              <li>
                  foreign particles in milk               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) coming from the outside
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/external">external</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/outside">outside</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  extraneous light in the camera spoiled the photograph               </li>
                              <li>
                  relying upon an extraneous income               </li>
                              <li>
                  disdaining outside pressure groups               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>

      <h2>extrapolation</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : projection; conjecture; V. extrapolate: infer (unknown information) from known information</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (mathematics) calculation of the value of a function outside the range of known values
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an inference about the future (or about some hypothetical situation) based on known facts and observations
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
