      <h2>vulgar</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : of the common people; deficient in refinement; not refined; coarse; Ex. vulgar display of wealth; N. vulgarism: vulgarity; crudely indecent word; CF. vulgarian: vulgar person; boor; lout</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) lacking refinement or cultivation or taste

                  behavior that branded him as common               </li>

      <h2>vulnerable</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) susceptible to attack
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a vulnerable bridge               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) susceptible to criticism or persuasion or temptation
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  vulnerable to bribery               </li>
                              <li>
                  an argument vulnerable to refutation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) capable of being wounded or hurt
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

                              <li>
