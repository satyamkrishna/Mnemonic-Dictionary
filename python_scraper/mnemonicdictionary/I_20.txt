      <h2>incidental</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not essential; minor; N: something incidental</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (frequently plural) an expense not budgeted or not specified
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/incidental expense">incidental expense</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/minor expense">minor expense</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he requested reimbursement of $7 for incidental expenses               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an item that is incidental
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (sometimes followed by `to') minor or casual or subordinate in significance or nature or occurring as a chance concomitant or consequence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/incident">incident</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  incidental expenses               </li>
                              <li>
                  the road will bring other incidental advantages               </li>
                              <li>
                  extra duties incidental to the job               </li>
                              <li>
                  labor problems incidental to a rapid expansion               </li>
                              <li>
                  confusion incidental to a quick change               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not of prime or central importance
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/nonessential">nonessential</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  nonessential to the integral meanings of poetry               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) following or accompanying as a consequence
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/accompanying">accompanying</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/attendant">attendant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/concomitant">concomitant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/consequent">consequent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ensuant">ensuant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resultant">resultant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sequent">sequent</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  an excessive growth of bureaucracy, with attendant problems               </li>
                              <li>
                  snags incidental to the changeover in management               </li>
                              <li>
                  attendant circumstances               </li>
                              <li>
                  the period of tension and consequent need for military preparedness               </li>
                              <li>
                  the ensuant response to his appeal               </li>
                              <li>
                  the resultant savings were considerable               </li>
                           </ul>
