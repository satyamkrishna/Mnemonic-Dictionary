      <h2>institution</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : instituting; (building for the) organization; established custom, practice, or relationship in a society; mental hospital; Ex. institution of marriage</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an organization founded and united for a specific purpose
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/establishment">establishment</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an establishment consisting of a building or complex of buildings where an organization for the promotion of some cause is situated
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a custom that for a long time has been an important feature of some group or society
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
