      <h2>demagogue</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : person who appeals to people's prejudice; false leader of people; CF. demagoguery</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular passions and prejudices
