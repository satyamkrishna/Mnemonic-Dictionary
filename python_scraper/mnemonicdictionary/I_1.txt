      <h2>iconoclastic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : attacking cherished traditions; N. iconoclast: one who attacks traditional ideas; one who destroys sacred images</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) characterized by attack on established beliefs or institutions
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) destructive of images used in religious worship; said of religions, such as Islam, in which the representation of living things is prohibited
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>idiom</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/parlance">parlance</a>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the usage or vocabulary that is characteristic of a specific group of people
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/accent">accent</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dialect">dialect</a>

            
             
         </div>
