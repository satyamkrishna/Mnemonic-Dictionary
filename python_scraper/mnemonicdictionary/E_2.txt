      <h2>eccentricity</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : oddity; idiosyncrasy</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) strange and unconventional behavior
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (geometry) a ratio describing the shape of a conic section; the ratio of the distance between the foci to the length of the major axis
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a circle is an ellipse with zero eccentricity               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a circularity that has a different center or deviates from a circular path
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>
