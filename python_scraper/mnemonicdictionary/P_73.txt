      <h2>purport</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : intention; purpose; meaning; V: claim; profess; Ex. order which purports to be signed by the general</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the intended meaning of a communication
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/intent">intent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/spirit">spirit</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the pervading meaning or tenor
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/drift">drift</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  caught the general drift of the conversation               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) have the often specious appearance of being, intending, or claiming
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>

         (verb) propose or intend

      <h2>purse</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : pucker; contract (lips) into wrinkles; N: wallet</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a container used for carrying money and small personal items or accessories (especially by women)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/bag">bag</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/handbag">handbag</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pocketbook">pocketbook</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  she reached into her bag and found a comb               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sum of money spoken of as the contents of a money purse
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he made the contribution out of his own purse               </li>
                              <li>
                  he and his wife shared a common purse               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a small bag for carrying money
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sum of money offered as a prize
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the purse barely covered the winner's expenses               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) contract one's lips into a rounded shape
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) gather or contract into wrinkles or folds; pucker
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/wrinkle">wrinkle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  purse ones's lips               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
