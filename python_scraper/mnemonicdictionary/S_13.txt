      <h2>season</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : enhance the flavor of by adding a spice, etc.; inure; harden; N. seasoning: something used in seasoning</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a period of the year marked by special events or activities in some field
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he celebrated his 10th season with the ballet company               </li>
                              <li>
                  she always looked forward to the avocado season               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) one of the natural periods into which the year is divided by the equinoxes and solstices or atmospheric conditions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/time of year">time of year</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the regular sequence of the seasons               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a recurrent time marked by major holidays
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  it was the Christmas season               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) lend flavor to
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flavor">flavor</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flavour">flavour</a>

            
             
         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make fit
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/harden">harden</a>
