      <h2>suspend</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : cause to stop for a period; interrupt; hold in abeyance; defer; hang from above; exclude for a period from a position; Ex. suspended state; Ex. suspend judgment; Ex. suspended from the team; N. suspen</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) hang freely
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The secret police suspended their victims from the ceiling and beat them               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to be held in suspension in a fluid
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  suspend the particles               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) bar temporarily; from school, office, etc.
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/debar">debar</a>

         <u>Definition</u><br />

                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/freeze">freeze</a>

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

      <h2>suspense</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : state of being undecided; anxiety or apprehension resulting from uncertainty</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an uncertain cognitive state
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) excited anticipation of an approaching climax
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the play kept the audience in suspense               </li>
