      <h2>complement</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : complete; consummate; make perfect; N.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a word or phrase used to complete a grammatical construction
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a complete number or quantity
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a full complement               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) number needed to make up a whole force
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/full complement">full complement</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a full complement of workers               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something added to complete or embellish or make perfect
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/accompaniment">accompaniment</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a fine wine is a perfect complement to the dinner               </li>
                              <li>
                  wild rice was served as an accompaniment to the main dish               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) one of a series of enzymes in the blood serum that are part of the immune response
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) either of two parts that mutually complete each other
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make complete or perfect; supply what is wanting or form the complement to
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I need some pepper to complement the sweet touch in the soup               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

               </div>

      <h2>complementary</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : serving to complete something</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) either one of two chromatic colors that when mixed together give white (in the case of lights) or grey (in the case of pigments)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/complementary color">complementary color</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  yellow and blue are complementaries               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) of words or propositions so related that each is the negation of the other
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  `male' and `female' are complementary terms               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) acting as or providing a complement (something that completes the whole)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/complemental">complemental</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/completing">completing</a>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) acting according to certain accepted standards
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/abidance">abidance</a>

         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a disposition or tendency to yield to the will of others
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

      <h2>component</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : element; ingredient</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an abstract part of something
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/constituent">constituent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/element">element</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/factor">factor</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/ingredient">ingredient</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  jealousy was a component of his character               </li>
                              <li>
                  two constituents of a musical composition are melody and harmony               </li>
                              <li>
                  the grammatical elements of a sentence               </li>
                              <li>
                  a key factor in her success               </li>
                              <li>
                  humor: an effective ingredient of a speech               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) something determined in relation to something that includes it
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/component part">component part</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/constituent">constituent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/part">part</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/portion">portion</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he wanted to feel a part of something bigger than himself               </li>
                              <li>
                  I read a portion of the manuscript               </li>
                              <li>
                  the smaller component is hard to reach               </li>
                              <li>
                  the animal constituent of plankton               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an artifact that is one of the individual parts of which a composite entity is made up; especially a part that can be separated from or attached to a system
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/constituent">constituent</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/element">element</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  spare components for cars               </li>

                  a component or constituent element of a system               </li>
