      <h2>saturnine</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : morose; gloomy; Ex. Do not be misled by his saturnine countenance.</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) bitter or scornful
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the face was saturnine and swarthy, and the sensual lips...twisted with disdain               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>satyr</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : half-human, half-bestial being in the court of Dionysus (resembling a goat), portrayed as wanton(unrestrained) and cunning; lecher; CF. faun; CF. goat: lecherous man</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) man with strong sexual desires
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lech">lech</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/lecher">lecher</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/letch">letch</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) one of a class of woodland deities; attendant on Bacchus; identified with Roman fauns
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forest god">forest god</a>
