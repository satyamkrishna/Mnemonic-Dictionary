      <h2>contentious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : quarrelsome; controversial; likely to cause arguments</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) inclined or showing an inclination to dispute or disagree, even to engage in law suits
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/combative">combative</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disputatious">disputatious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disputative">disputative</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/litigious">litigious</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a style described as abrasive and contentious               </li>
                              <li>
                  a disputatious lawyer               </li>
                              <li>
                  a litigious and acrimonious spirit               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) involving or likely to cause controversy
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a central and contentious element of the book               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
