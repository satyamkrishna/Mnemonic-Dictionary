      <h2>pulsate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : throb; beat regularly; vibrate regularly</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) expand and contract rhythmically; beat rhythmically
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pulse">pulse</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/throb">throb</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The baby's heart was pulsating again after the surgeon massaged it               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) move with or as if with a regular alternating motion
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/beat">beat</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/quiver">quiver</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the city pulsated with music and excitement               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) produce or modulate (as electromagnetic waves) in the form of short bursts or pulses or cause an apparatus to produce pulses
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pulse">pulse</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

      <h2>pulse</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : rhythmical throbbing of arteries; brief sudden change in a normally constant quantity; V: pulsate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (electronics) a sharp transient wave in the normal electrical state (or a series of such transients)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/impulse">impulse</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pulsation">pulsation</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pulsing">pulsing</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  the pulsations seemed to be coming from a star               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the rhythmic contraction and expansion of the arteries with each beat of the heart
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/beat">beat</a>

                        ,

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the rate at which the heart beats; usually measured to obtain a quick evaluation of a person's health
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/heart rate">heart rate</a>
