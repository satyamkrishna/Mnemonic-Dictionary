      <h2>resilient</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : elastic; having the power of springing back; able to recover quickly (as from misfortune)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) recovering readily from adversity, depression, or the like
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) elastic; rebounds readily

      <h2>resolution</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : determination; resoluteness; ADJ. resolute: firm or determined in purpose</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a formal expression by a meeting; agreed to by a vote
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/declaration">declaration</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resolve">resolve</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the ability of a microscope or telescope to measure the angular separation of images that are close together
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resolving power">resolving power</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the trait of being resolute
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/firmness">firmness</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/firmness of purpose">firmness of purpose</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resoluteness">resoluteness</a>

                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resolve">resolve</a>
