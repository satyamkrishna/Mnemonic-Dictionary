      <h2>summation</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : act of finding the total; summing-up; summary (esp. one given by the judge at the end of a trial)</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a concluding summary (as in presenting a case before a law court)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rundown">rundown</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/summing up">summing up</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (physiology) the process whereby multiple stimuli can produce a response (in a muscle or nerve or other part) that one stimulus alone does not produce
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the final aggregate
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sum">sum</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sum total">sum total</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the sum of all our troubles did not equal the misery they suffered               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the arithmetic operation of summing; calculating the sum of two or more numbers
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/addition">addition</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/plus">plus</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the summation of four and three gives seven               </li>
                              <li>
                  four plus three equals seven               </li>
                           </ul>
