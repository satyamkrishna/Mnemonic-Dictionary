      <h2>tortuous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : winding; full of curves; Ex. tortuous mountain road</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) highly complex or intricate and occasionally devious
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/byzantine">byzantine</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/convoluted">convoluted</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/involved">involved</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/knotty">knotty</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tangled">tangled</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the Byzantine tax structure               </li>
                              <li>
                  Byzantine methods for holding on to his chairmanship               </li>
                              <li>
                  convoluted legal language               </li>
                              <li>
                  convoluted reasoning               </li>
                              <li>
                  the plot was too involved               </li>
                              <li>
                  a knotty problem               </li>
                              <li>
                  got his way by labyrinthine maneuvering               </li>
                              <li>
                  Oh, what a tangled web we weave               </li>
                              <li>
                  tortuous legal procedures               </li>
                              <li>
                  tortuous negotiations lasting for months               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) marked by repeated turns and bends
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twisting">twisting</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/twisty">twisty</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/voluminous">voluminous</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/winding">winding</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a tortuous road up the mountain               </li>
                              <li>
                  winding roads are full of surprises               </li>
                              <li>
                  had to steer the car down a twisty track               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) not straightforward
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  his tortuous reasoning               </li>

      <h2>toss</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : throw lightly; move or lift (the head) with a sudden motion; flip (a coin) to decide something</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of flipping a coin
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flip">flip</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (sports) the act of throwing the ball to another member of your team
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flip">flip</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pass">pass</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  the pass was fumbled               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an abrupt movement
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a toss of his head               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) throw or toss with a light motion
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/flip">flip</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pitch">pitch</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/sky">sky</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  flip me the beachball               </li>
                              <li>
                  toss me newspaper               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />
