      <h2>vortex</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : whirlwind; whirlpool; center of turbulence; predicament into which one is inexorably plunged</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the shape of something rotating rapidly
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/convolution">convolution</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/swirl">swirl</a>

                        ,

         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a powerful circular current of water (usually the result of conflicting tides)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
