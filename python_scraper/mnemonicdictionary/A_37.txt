      <h2>anticlimax</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a disappointing decline after a previous rise
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
