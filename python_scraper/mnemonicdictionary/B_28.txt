      <h2>breadth</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : width; extent</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the capacity to understand a broad range of topics
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/comprehensiveness">comprehensiveness</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/largeness">largeness</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a teacher must have a breadth of knowledge of the subject               </li>
                              <li>
                  a man distinguished by the largeness and scope of his views               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the extent of something from side to side
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/width">width</a>

         (noun) a special variety of domesticated animals within a species

      <h2>bridle</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : harness fitted about a horse's head (used to restrain); curb; check; V: put a bridle on; control or restrain; show anger</p>

         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
