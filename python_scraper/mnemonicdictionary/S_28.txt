      <h2>singular</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : being only one; individual; unique; extraordinary; odd; Ex. singular beauty/behavior</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the form of a word that is used to denote a singleton
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/singular form">singular form</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) unusual or striking
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/remarkable">remarkable</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a remarkable sight               </li>
                              <li>
                  such poise is singular in one so young               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) beyond or deviating from the usual or expected
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/curious">curious</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/funny">funny</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/odd">odd</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/peculiar">peculiar</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/queer">queer</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rum">rum</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/rummy">rummy</a>

            
             
         </div>

            <ul>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being a single and separate person or thing
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  can the singular person be understood apart from his culture?               </li>

         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) composed of one member, set, or kind
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) grammatical number category referring to a single item or unit
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) the single one of its kind

         </div>
