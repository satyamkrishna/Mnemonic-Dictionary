      <h2>pique</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : irritation; resentment from wounded pride (eg. loss in a contest); V: provoke; arouse; annoy; cause to feel resentment; Ex. pique her curiosity</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) tightly woven fabric with raised cords
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a sudden outburst of anger
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/irritation">irritation</a>

                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause to feel resentment or indignation

            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/offend">offend</a>
