      <h2>bucolic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : rustic; pastoral</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a country person
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/peasant">peasant</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/provincial">provincial</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a short poem descriptive of rural or pastoral life
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/eclogue">eclogue</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/idyl">idyl</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/idyll">idyll</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) (used with regard to idealized country life) idyllically rustic
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/arcadian">arcadian</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pastoral">pastoral</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a country life of arcadian contentment               </li>
                              <li>
                  a pleasant bucolic scene               </li>
                              <li>
                  charming in its pastoral setting               </li>
                              <li>
                  rustic tranquility               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) relating to shepherds or herdsmen or devoted to raising sheep or cattle
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pastoral">pastoral</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  pastoral seminomadic people               </li>
                              <li>
                  pastoral land               </li>
                              <li>
                  a pastoral economy               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>bulge</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : protruding part; swelling of a surface; Ex. The apple made a bulge in his pocket; V: swell outward; protrude</p>
