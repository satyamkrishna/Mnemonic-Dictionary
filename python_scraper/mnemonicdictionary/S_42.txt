      <h2>soporific</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : sleep-causing; marked by sleepiness; drowsy</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a drug that induces sleep
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
