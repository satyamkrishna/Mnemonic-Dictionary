      <h2>drip</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : fall or let fall in drops; shed drops; N: action or sound of falling in drops; liquid that falls in drops</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) flowing in drops; the formation and falling of drops of liquid
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dribble">dribble</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/trickle">trickle</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  there's a drip through the roof               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the sound of a liquid falling drop by drop
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/dripping">dripping</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (architecture) a projection from a cornice or sill designed to protect the area below from rainwater (as over a window or doorway)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :
