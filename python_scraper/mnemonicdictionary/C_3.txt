      <h2>callous</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hardened; unfeeling; without sympathy for the sufferings of others; unkind</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make insensitive or callous; deaden feelings or morals
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cauterise">cauterise</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cauterize">cauterize</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) emotionally hardened
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/indurate">indurate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pachydermatous">pachydermatous</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a callous indifference to suffering               </li>
                              <li>
                  cold-blooded and indurate to public opinion               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having calluses; having skin made tough and thick through wear
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/calloused">calloused</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/thickened">thickened</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  calloused skin               </li>
                              <li>
                  with a workman's callous hands               </li>
                           </ul>

      <h2>callus</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : area of thick hard skin</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) an area of skin that is thick or hard from continual pressure or friction (as the sole of the foot)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/callosity">callosity</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) bony tissue formed during the healing of a fractured bone
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (botany) an isolated thickening of tissue, especially a stiff protuberance on the lip of an orchid
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) cause a callus to form on
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  The long march had callused his feet               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) form a callus or calluses
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  His foot callused               </li>
                           </ul>
