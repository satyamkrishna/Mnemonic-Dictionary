      <h2>insinuate</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : hint; imply; suggest indirectly; creep in; introduce or insert (oneself) by artful means; Ex. insinuate himself into the boss's favor; CF. ingratiate</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) introduce or insert (oneself) in a subtle manner
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  He insinuated himself into the conversation of the people at the nearby table               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) give to understand
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/adumbrate">adumbrate</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/intimate">intimate</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I insinuated that I did not like his wife               </li>
                           </ul>
