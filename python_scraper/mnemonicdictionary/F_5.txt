      <h2>fashion</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : give shape to; make; Ex. fashion the pot out of clay</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) how something is done or how it happens
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/manner">manner</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/mode">mode</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/style">style</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/way">way</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  her dignified manner               </li>
                              <li>
                  his rapid manner of talking               </li>
                              <li>
                  their nomadic mode of existence               </li>
                              <li>
                  in the characteristic New York style               </li>
                              <li>
                  a lonely way of life               </li>
                              <li>
                  in an abrasive fashion               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) characteristic or habitual practice
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the latest and most admired style in clothes and cosmetics and behavior
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) consumer goods (especially clothing) in the current mode
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) make out of components (often in an improvising manner)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forge">forge</a>

      <h2>fastidious</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : difficult to please; squeamish; fussy; finicky</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) giving careful attention to detail; hard to please; excessively concerned with cleanliness
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  a fastidious and incisive intellect               </li>
                              <li>
                  fastidious about personal cleanliness               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) having complicated nutritional requirements; especially growing only in special artificial cultures
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/exacting">exacting</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  fastidious microorganisms               </li>
                              <li>
                  certain highly specialized xerophytes are extremely exacting in their requirements               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
