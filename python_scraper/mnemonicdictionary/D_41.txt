      <h2>dissent</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : disagree</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) (law) the difference of one judge's opinion from that of the majority
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  he expressed his dissent in a contrary opinion               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a difference of opinion
         
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the act of protesting; a public (often organized) manifestation of dissent
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/objection">objection</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/protest">protest</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) withhold assent
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  Several Republicans dissented               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) express opposition through action or words
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/protest">protest</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/resist">resist</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  dissent to the laws of the country               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (verb) be of different opinions
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/differ">differ</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/disagree">disagree</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/take issue">take issue</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  I beg to differ!               </li>
                              <li>
                  She disagrees with her husband on many questions               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
