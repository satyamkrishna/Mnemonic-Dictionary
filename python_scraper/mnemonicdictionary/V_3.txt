      <h2>vanguard</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : forerunners; foremost position of an army; advance forces; foremost position in a trend or movement; CF. rearguard</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the leading units moving at the head of an army
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/van">van</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) any creative group active in the innovation and application of new concepts and techniques in a given field (especially in the arts)
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/avant-garde">avant-garde</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/new wave">new wave</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/van">van</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the position of greatest importance or advancement; the leading position in any movement or field
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/cutting edge">cutting edge</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/forefront">forefront</a>

            
             
         </div>
