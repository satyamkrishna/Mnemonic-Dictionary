      <h2>pedagogy</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : teaching; art of education</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the principles and methods of instruction
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/pedagogics">pedagogics</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/teaching method">teaching method</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) the profession of a teacher
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/instruction">instruction</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/teaching">teaching</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  he prepared for teaching while still in college               </li>

                  pedagogy is recognized as an important profession               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>
