      <h2>narrative</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : related to telling a story; N: narrated account; story; V. narrate: tell (a story); CF. narration</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a message that tells the particulars of an act or occurrence or course of events; presented in writing or drama or cinema or as a radio or television program
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">
            <u>Synonyms</u> :           
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/narration">narration</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/story">story</a>

                        ,
            
                        <a href="http://www.mnemonicdictionary.com/word/tale">tale</a>

            
             
         </div>
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>

                  his narrative was interesting               </li>
