      <h2>exotic</h2>
      <p><strong>Short Definition</strong> : not native; from another part of the world; strange; intriguingly unusual; Ex. exotic flower/dress</p>

         
      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) being or from or characteristic of another place or part of the world
