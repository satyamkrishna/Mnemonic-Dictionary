      <h2>pristine</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) completely free from dirt or contamination
         
          

                 
         <div style="padding-top: 10px;"><u>Example Sentence</u> <br />
            <ul>
                              <li>
                  pristine mountain snow               </li>
                           </ul>
         </div>
            
         <hr style="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:5px;"/>

         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (adj) immaculately clean and unused

      <h2>privation</h2>

      <div style="padding-bottom:0px;">
         

         <u>Definition</u><br />      
         (noun) a state of extreme poverty
         
                  <div style="padding-top:8px;">

                        ,

         </div>

         <u>Definition</u><br />
